AST has a Pass Through Co-op program called "AST Allowance." Resellers enrolled in the AST Allowance program will be compensated at a rate of 1% of their total net purchases of AST products. Purchases must be made through an AST National Channel Account.

Use your funds to create the extraordinary ad campaigns, incredible direct mailings or awesome sales promotions you've been wanting to do, but have not had the funds to do so until now. AST will even provide free ad materials such as line art and AST logo sheets.

Here's how the AST Allowance program works:

  1. Participants must be Resellers. Any Reseller selling AST product may participate.
  2. Buy your AST products through any participating AST National Channel Account as usual. Simply check with your preferred National Channel Account for program details.
  3. Register your company's information with AST's Processing Center, CoAMS. Complete the attached enrollment form, include a copy of your Resale Certificate and fax to:
    CoAMS
    Attn: AST Allowance Program
    FAX (312) 243-2531
  4. Your AST purchases will be tracked at the National Channel Account's corporate headquarters and sent to AST's Processing Center monthly.
  5. AST will send you a check in the amount of 1% of your total net AST purchases each month. Your first AST Allowance check will be sent directly to you following the month of your enrollment with CoAMS.
  6. There are no prior approvals, no claim forms, no hassle.
