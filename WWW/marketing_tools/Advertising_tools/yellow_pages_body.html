Yellow Pages Advertisements

Yellow Pages advertising is a well known lead resource for the computer reseller industry, and it has been for years. It is an advertising medium that works for you 365 days a years, 7 days a week and 24 hours a day.

Understanding the value of this medium, AST has developed a program in conjunction with YPM, Inc. of Irvine, CA, to provide free yellow pages trade name advertisements for each Ovation! Gold Reseller within their primary directory.

Advertisements will be placed under the heading of “Computer-Dealers” or such similar headings.

YPM is a specialized licensed advertising agency that structures tailored and sophisticated yellow page programs for firms doing business nationally and internationally. YPM will design, implement and coordinate all directly placements. YPM maintains all the necessary tools to coordinate all of your placements.

We strongly believe that this yellow pages advertisement program will enhance your ability to further your sales growth and successfully grow your business.

To participate, please complete the following request form. YPM will contact you to verify receipt and coordinate your advertisement.

Should you have any questions or wish to receive additional information, please contact YPM at (800) 877-8223 and ask for the AST Computer Program Manager.
