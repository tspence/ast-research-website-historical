Minimum Requirements

Sales
Maintain annual sales of $2 million in AST commercial systems products.

Contact Resource
AST Ovation! Gold Resellers must maintain at least one contact in each of the following functional areas: Sales, technical, and leads.

Reporting
AST Ovation! Gold Resellers must provide monthly sales out reporting and channel inventory reports.

Marketing
Ovation! Gold Resellers must participate in a minimum of one marketing activity per quarter.
