Introduction

AST Research, Inc. announces the latest 1996 edition of the Authorized Service Center Handbook - How the Americas Do Business with AST. This handbook provides you with reference material that encompasses the most current service policies, procedures, programs, and product information of AST Research, Inc.

The ASC Handbook has been designed to provide you with a comprehensive, yet concise source on how to do business with AST Service. Our goal is to ensure that your experiences with AST Service are always handled professionally and in a timely manner.

The format gives us the flexibility to easily update sections as required, and every effort has been made to organize the information in such a way that all related materials appear in common sections for ease of use.
