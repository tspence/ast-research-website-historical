Flash BIOS Tables

Use the tables in this area to see the latest revision of your BIOS, and to download the update files to your system. This page was designed for Web browsers which support FTP. Browsers which do not support FTP may generate an error message when attempting to download these files.

In order to use these tables, you must know the part number of your computer. AST system part numbers are six digits long, and always begin with a "5". Some examples are "501568" and "500996". You will find this part number either on the back of the system, or on the bottom. On many portable units, the part number is only viewable if you first remove the battery.

If you do not find your system part number on these tables, then either:

  1. Your BIOS is not Flash Upgradeable. You must order hardware to upgrade.
  2. Your BIOS is the latest available. An upgrade has not been released.
