Pronto!Pro Online

Quick Start Handbook

  

a. Introduction

Welcome to the next generation of Pronto!Pro: Pronto!ProOnline, the complete AST product information tool, now accessible over the internet, worldwide, 24 hours a day. This document will be your quick start guide and reference manual for using the new Pronto!ProOnline system efficiently, and at the greatest ease possible. Although Pronto!ProOnline was written to be as simple as possible, it is suggested you read this document for a general overview of all of its features and components.

 

1. Overview/Interface Tutorial

Pronto!Pro Online is the new World Wide Web adapted version of AST’s standard product support and information tool, Pronto!Pro. This new online version has been created with ease in mind, as well as efficient use. The interface itself, has been modeled after Microsoft Windows and its file system, the explorer. Through the use of this explorer interface, files and information are handled in folders in a hierarchy tree form. To gain access to any information you may be looking for, you simple break the tree down by clicking on the folders named in relationship to that information.

Ex 1. Simple Information Retrieval : Customer wants information on – 17" 7L Monitor.

Step 1.) Select the Products icon from the Menu Bar on the Pronto!ProOnline homepage.

Step 2.) Narrow your search by selecting the Monitors icon from left-hand frame in your browser.

Step 3.) Narrow your search a bit more by selecting 17" Monitors from the expanded folder tree.

Step 4.) Finally, you may select the 7L monitor that suites your customers needs from the list of available 17" monitors on the right-hand frame of your browser.

Note: More examples are avaliable for each section of Pronto!ProOnline

 

2. Main Menu Bar

The Menu bar of Pronto!Pro Online contains 8 main icons which operate similar to any other icon on your browsers various other toolbars. Products, BIOS, ASC, Tech Ref, News, Help, Tools, and Feedback. Each button is a link to a separate section of the Pronto!ProOnline System. The Buttons work as followed:

a. Products

The products section is the core functionality of the New Pronto!ProOnline system. By clicking on the Products Icon, the left-hand frame of your browser will be filled with a top down heiarchy view of AST’s entire product line including systems and monitors, in one easy to use visual tree. This tree is organized and built with the functionality to emulate the windows 95 explorer interface. By simply clicking on the folder of your choice the tree explodes to reveal its contents, and by repeating this action you can narrow your search to any product you may be searching for.

b. BIOS Tables

By clicking the BIOS icon, you are brought into the BIOS Table section of Pronto!ProOnline. Much like the Products section, the left-hand frame is modeled after the windows 95 explorer, and reacts in the same general way. By clicking on the product line of which BIOS you are in search of, the right-hand frame will fill with a quick reference table of BIOS files available for that product line. By clicking on the filename in the "BIOS Update File" field of the product BIOS table you will be prompted to download that BIOS file to your local hard disk drive.

c. ASC Handbook

By clicking on the ASC button in the main menu bar, your left-hand browser frame will change to a tree of folders each entitled to a corresponding regions, and each containing the full text in PDF format of the AST ASC Handbook for that country. Simply click on your regions title to download the ASC Handbook to your local hard disk drive.

d. Technical Reference

The Tech Ref icons leads you into the AST Technical Reference section of www.ast.com. Just click on one of the three icons in the left-hand frame of your browser, and you will be directly linked in to all of your AST bulletin needs.

e. News

The News icon leads you to a simple view of all news bulletins relating to Pronto!ProOnline and its continual development and additions.

f. Tools

The Tools icon leads you into the software tools and links section of Pronto!ProOnline. Within this section you can download just about any tool you may need for navigating around the web based version of Pronto!Pro. This section of Pronto!ProOnline also contains links to popular hardware and software vendor homepages in which may have other useful information related to AST product support.

g. Help

You’re looking at it. Come here for help, tutorials, tips, general information, future news, a glossary, and the site map for Pronto!ProOnline.

h. Feedback

Select the Feedback icon of the Pronto!ProOnline menu bar to submit all comments regarding Pronto!ProOnline, it’s bugs, ideas for it’s further development, or just senseless praise to its wonderful creators for making your life so much simpler.

  2. BIOS Tables

    3. Example, Downloading BIOS : You need to download the bios for Bravo MS-166 (503418).

    Step 1.) Select the BIOS icon from the Menu Bar on the Pronto!ProOnline homepage.

    Step 2.) Narrow your search by selecting the type of system BIOS from the left-hand frame of your browser. This will align the right-hand frame to the section of BIOS you selected. In this case, the selection would be Bravo.

    Step 3.) Now drag the right-hand frame to the matching row of the BIOS table matching your needs. Drag the table until you reach 503418, Bravo MS-166.

    Step 4.) Next click the filename** in the far right cell of the current row to download your BIOS file. In this case - 05606036.EXE

    Step 5.) Uncompress the file by executing it to an empty directory, and read any further instructions contained within.

    ** note: All BIOS filenames will end with .EXE.

    Step 3.) By clicking on the link, you will be brought to the software companies download section where you can download the file specifically for your system.
