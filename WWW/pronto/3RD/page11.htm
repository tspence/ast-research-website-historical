Vendor Product Description:

The Tecmar Technologies TS3900i is a DDS-3 tape drive, has a 3.5" form factor and embedded SCSI-2 controller.  24Gb (assuming 2:1 data compression) or 12 Gb capacity (compression disabled) per 125m DDS-3 cartridge.  The increased linear density combines with new LSI's and precise control system to deliver a sustained data transfer rate of 2.4Mb/sec. (assuming 2:1 compression).  Ity provides a capacity to transfer rate of 10,000:1, making it possible to meet the requirements of MIS managers for a shorter backup time.

Test Results:

The Tecmar tape drive has been tested under NT 4.0 and Netware 4.11.  NT 4.0 and Netware 4.11 were both tested on the Manhattan D & S Series.  Tests run were as follows:

1.  Full backup, selective restore.
2.  Scheduled backup.
3.  Immediate backup.

Configuration Note:

On the S6200 Cheyenne Arcserve for NT and Netware was able to detect the drive, see the tape, erase and format but was unable to perform a scheduled or immediate backup.  The following errors were displayed every time: 1. E6300 Windows NT SCSI port error [ABSL:0240 CMD:1 1h NT:45D].  2. E3855 Unable to position tape (Tape=Test1, EC=Hardware Error).
