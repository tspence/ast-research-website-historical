ASCENTIA™ P SERIES
Models P70/P80
The Premium Notebook for Power and Performance. 



















The enhanced Ascentia P Series notebook continues to deliver the latest in 

mobile computing technology.  The Ascentia P comes equipped with either a 150MHz or 166MHz Intel® Pentium® Processor with MMX™ technology for incredible power and performance, plus maximized multimedia capabilities. 


















Key Features:



    

  • 150MHz or 166MHz Intel® Pentium® processor with MMX™ technology and 256KB level-2 cache

  • Large Super VGA 12.1" diagonal TFT Active-Matrix color screen

  • Modular 10X Maximim Speed CD-ROM drive standard

  • Integrated 28.8kbps data/fax modem (U.S. and Canada only)

  • SmartPoint™ touchpad pointing device 

  • 3.0 or 2.1GB Hard Drive

  • Switch between a CD-ROM drive, floppy drive or optional second battery in the AST "SmartBay"

  •  Multimedia support with 16-bit integrated sound, built-in microphone and dual stereo speakers

  •  Integrated infrared port
