Advantage! 9312 Communications PC.

Now, distant family members are only a keystroke away! Bridge that gap and keep in touch face-to-face with
the Advantage! 9312 Communications PC. Featuring state-of-the-art communications technology, the
Advantage! 9312 is the complete interactive communications PC with Intel Video Phone technology and an
analog video camera that allows you to see family and friends using a standard phone line. The system also
offers Intercast technology from Intel, which combines the rich programming of TV and the exciting world
of the Internet. This feature will allow users watching CNN, for example, to download information on the
topic that is being broadcast and learn on the spot or save to read later.
