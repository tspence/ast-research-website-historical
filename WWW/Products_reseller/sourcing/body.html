National Channel Accounts

AST’s National Channel Accounts (NCAs) offer a variety of programs and services designed to meet the needs of Indirect Resellers. The NCAs are subdivided into two categories: Master Resellers, a two-tiered model, and Systems Integrators, serving corporate end users.
