AST established by Albert Wong, Safi Qureshey and Tom Yuen to produce enhancement boards for the original IBM; PCs, improving system speed and flexibility.

The company enjoyed early success and by the time AST introduced its first personal computer, AST already had a reputation for quality, innovative technology, manufacturing expertise and superior service.

AST completed a strategic alliance with Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd, South Korea's largest electronics company. The agreements result in an investment of approximately 49 percent in AST, as well as other strategic relationships, including component supply and joint product development, OEM arrangements and cross-licensing of patents.

AST designs, develops, manufactures and markets desktop, server and notebook computers that deliver value and performance to a variety of users. These feature-rich, award-winning products combine high performance with competitive pricing to create outstanding long-term value. Product families include the Bravo desktops and minitowers, Ascentia notebooks and Manhattan servers, with models suitable for almost every kind of business or personal application and budget.



As the PC industry becomes more competitive, AST's goal is to be recognized and respected worldwide. The company is dedicated to providing best-of-class information tools that are easy to learn and operate, affordable and available through the most popular and convenient locations.
