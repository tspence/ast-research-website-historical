Increased Customer Recognition
Increasing brand awareness for AST also remains a key objective towards increasing sales.  This year, the company has significantly increased its worldwide advertising efforts in print, radio and broadcast mediums to bolster recognition of the AST name and to communicate the company’s distinguishing product features from competitors.  Plans for 1998 and beyond call for sustained growth in advertising and other marketing-related activities.

“The AST brand name will continue to be used in the worldwide locations where it is being used now,” added Kim.  “AST has a loyal customer base and a reputation for innovation and value that we intend to grow even stronger over the years.”

Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd., a $19 billion company of the $93 billion Samsung Group, is a world leader in electronics, with operations in more than 60 countries.  The company challenges itself to make consumer electronics, semiconductors, communications and industrial electronics that are effective, useful, simple and powerful.  Samsung Electronics employs over 80,000 people worldwide.

AST Computer develops and markets a broad spectrum of desktop, mobile and server PC products that are sold in more than 100 countries worldwide.  AST systems meet a wide range of business needs, from traditional corporate business applications to customized small business programs.
