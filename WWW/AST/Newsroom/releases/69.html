AST PAVES WAY FOR INTERNET OF THE FUTURE WITH BEST-OF-CLASS WEB SOFTWARE TOOLS

AST-IntraAccess Standard On New Bravo MS-T Pentium® Pro Processor-Based Commercial Desktops

IRVINE, Calif., July 22, 1996 -- AST Computer (NASDAQ-ASTA) today strengthened its position as being first-to-market with leading edge technology with its first-of-a-kind AST-IntraAccess™ software bundle. Now shipping on new Bravo MS-T Pentium® Pro processor-based commercial desktops, AST-IntraAccess provides commercial customers with a complete network browsing and Web authoring software bundle, and includes all of the software that is necessary to make sharing data via a company Intranet a breeze.

AST-IntraAccess represents the latest addition to AST-CommandCenter, the company's third generation of desktop utilities, optimized and tuned for the Microsoft Windows® NT Workstation environment. With AST-IntraAccess, business users are able to extend communications within their business and internal organizational enterprises. Commercial organizations and individuals looking to effectively and efficiently harness the Internet and Intranet today now have the power at their fingertips.

"We have taken the commercial PC to the next dimension by incorporating software that will be critical for conducting business in an increasingly Web-centric environment," stated AST's Laura Raybin, director of product marketing for commercial desktops. "Staying on the cusp of information is critical to making strategic and important business decisions, and AST-IntraAccess helps give users that leading edge."

Included in the AST-IntraAccess toolkit are NETCOMplete™, Internet Factory's Communication Builder™, Netscape Navigator™ and HoTMetaL™ Light from SoftQuad.

"We are very pleased to be including HoTMetaL Light with quality AST products," said Dave Gurney, Chairman and CEO of SoftQuad. "By bundling HoTMetaL Light with AST's Bravo desktop computer, we continue to supply high-powered solutions for creating dynamic Web pages for Internets and corporate Intranets."

AST recognizes that integrating these superior software tools with the newest Bravo desktops enables users to more fully utilize the burgeoning growth of both Internet and Intranet software applications.

"We recognize that one of the most compelling reasons for utilizing the Internet and Intranet is the ability of software tools, such as those contained within AST-IntraAccess, to more fully and completely support commercial applications," noted Raybin. "This in turn extends the existing applications investments made by business customers today."

Similar to the thinking of several technology analysts today, AST is of the opinion that worldwide business use of Internet/Intranet software is exploding.

"We know that in 1996 approximately $15 million dollars will be spent by commercial users on Internet/Intranet software," Raybin stated. "We envision this figure growing to well over $75 million by the end of this century."

Sweeping The Globe
AST believes that the Internet revolution is sweeping the globe, and AST-IntraAccess helps make it possible for small, medium and large-sized businesses to have an almost instant presence on the Internet. The company believes that there are between 10 and 12 million users now accessing the World Wide Web, with more than fifty percent of large corporations having daily access to the Web.

"We think it is quite feasible that by the end of this year more than seventy-five percent of large corporations will be doing business on the Web," noted Raybin. "With AST-IntraAccess, an organization can more effectively manage the problem of information overload - because recipients can access exactly that specific information relevant to them."

All-In-One Tool Kit
With AST-IntraAccess, making an organization's information available electronically is dramatically more efficient. Electronic information can be easily disseminated, stored, altered, updated and even accessed on a large scale.

"We have determined that the amount of time a user spends looking for critical information, let's say competitive tracking statistics, can be reduced by as much as much as twenty percent with an in-house Intranet solution," stated Raybin. "What this means is that both the customers and employees benefit."

An Array of Business-Enhancing WEB Software
AST-IntraAccess gives users all of the necessary pieces to be up and running in a WEB environment in very little time. Combining these software tools into one bundle saves users potentially hundreds of dollars off of the retail price for each software product if purchased separately.

NETCOM Internet Service
AST's preferred Internet Service Provider (ISP) is NETCOM. As the nation's leading commercial ISP, NETCOM provides full service Internet connections to corporate users, as well as host dial-up service to individual and small business customers.

NETCOMplete
As a complete Internet kit, NETCOMplete includes a wide variety of the most popular software available. NETCOMplete includes a protocol stack and dialer with an intuitive Windows interface that lets you point and click through a full range of services including electronic mail, the World Wide Web, news groups and "chat rooms."

Netscape Navigator
Surfing the Internet for the latest stock information and technology trend watching is quick and hassle-free with Netscape's Navigator, widely recognized as the best Web browser available.

This incredible application supports both HTML 3.0 and multiple documents so that you can access the latest in Web graphics and open multiple windows simultaneously.

Internet Factory's Communications Builder
Creating a home page on the World Wide Web as well as creating an internal Web or Intranet is a snap with Communications Builder. Communications Builder acts as an HTTP proxy server, or "gateway" to pass information between an organization's internal network and the Internet. Acting as a proxy server, multiple requests for the same document can be fulfilled with a local cache, eliminating the need to traverse the network. When acting as a "gateway", Communications Builder allows for other resources to access the Internet via the server where the gateway is located.

HoTMetaL Light
Authoring Web Internet/Intranet pages is painless with SoftQuad's HoTMetaL Light authoring tool, helping users to be creative and get their message out in new and clever ways.

With an internal "home page," known as Intranet, users can share policies, procedures and reference manuals online.

The Web page creation features of HoTMetaL Light helps you do all of this in conjunction with Communications Builder.
