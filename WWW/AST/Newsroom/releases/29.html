PC SERVICESOURCE TO PROVIDE REAL-TIME INTERNET
WARRANTY SERVICES FOR AST® COMPUTER

AST Service Providers Gain Ground In Efficiency With Real-Time Parts Ordering

DALLAS, April 2, 1997 -- PC ServiceSource today announced that it will provide real-time, on-line warranty services to service providers and resellers of AST Computer products. Warranty transactions will be processed through PC ServiceSource's pc service net, the computer industry's first real-time Internet-based parts processing system.

Through pc service net, AST service providers will be able to file paperless warranty claims and do real-time parts ordering directly from their desktop via the Internet. AST service providers can also order non-warranty parts through pc service net, a service that has been available since August 1996.

"With pc service net, AST service providers will reap the benefit of real-time parts ordering by streamlining the time to respond to customer needs," said AST's Matt Doyle, vice president of service. "Additionally, being able to track the warranty claim directly from a desktop gives service providers new levels of control. By working with PC ServiceSource, AST is empowering its service providers and resellers to be best-in-class and highly responsive."

Steve Grimmer, PC ServiceSource's vice president of OEM services, stated "We are pleased that AST has chosen pc service net as their platform for real-time warranty parts ordering and claims processing. pc service net will provide benefits to the service providers and resellers with real-time access to parts availability and electronic filing of warranty claim forms."

Launched last fall, pc service net became the computer industry's first real-time online ordering system. PC ServiceSource's customers are able to check real-time availability of parts, determine the status of pending orders and enter new orders for computer parts. As anticipated, this advanced system is now being utilized to provide efficient warranty processing.

Providing 30 different lines of parts to a worldwide customer base exceeding 20,000 active accounts, PC ServiceSource (NASDAQ-PCSS) is the largest supplier of service logistics to service providers and original equipment manufacturers in the PC industry. Cyclix Engineering, the Company's remanufacturing subsidiary, offers a range of services which strategically complement the distribution-oriented service logistics offerings of PC ServiceSource. PC ServiceSource recently launched pc service net, the PC industry's first real-time Internet ordering and information retrieval system for spare parts. General and financial data is located on the Internet at www.pcservice.com.
