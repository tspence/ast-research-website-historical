HOME OFFICE ENTREPRENEURS GET MORE BANG FOR THE BUCK WITH LATEST AST ADVANTAGE! PC SYSTEM

AST Offers Technology-Packed Home Office PC to Value-Conscious Consumer

IRVINE, Calif., August 26, 1996 -- Imagine increasing your productivity and decreasing the time required to complete important tasks while working from home. Today that is possible with the AST Computer (NASDAQ:ASTA) Advantage! Home Office PC, now shipping.

Recognizing the time constraints on homeworkers and the highly desired goal of efficiency, the Advantage! 9310 Home Office PC tackles these needs with specialized features for home office applications. By bundling an easy-to-use full-page personal document scanner, a 33.6 Kbps Data/Fax/Voice modem and an infrared data communications port with a powerful AST consumer PC, working from home has never been easier.

While offering a bevy of hardware and software features, the new Advantage! 9310 also meets the needs of the entire family, from balancing the checkbook and providing family entertainment to surfing the Internet for timely reference information.

Responding to Consumer's Needs
"The home market has changed dramatically from five years ago. AST realizes that this fast-growing segment has evolved to include multi-users within the family as well as home businesses," said Terry Baker, director of marketing, worldwide consumer products. "Research projections indicate that by the end of the decade, there will be roughly 120 million PCs in homes in the U.S. We know that homeworker households account for 68 percent of the total consumer PC installed base and view this as an incredible opportunity for specialized systems."

Research from IDC/Link shows that work at home is increasing across the board for all types of home workers. Corporate downsizing has left a lot of highly skilled and qualified workers out of the traditional corporate work force. Many of these workers are making use of their skills by becoming independent contractors and freelancers, giving rise to small home-based businesses. IDC/Link's research has shown that 73 percent of home office households planning to purchase a PC within a year will buy a multimedia computer. The lower cost of acquisition of key platforms and services is further fueling the adoption of information and communications technologies by "technopreneurs." Since home office "technopreneurs" are the first to migrate to new technology, this opens up the window of opportunity for more PCs that are aimed at diverging and segmenting consumers.

Leading the Pack with Hot New Technologies
The new Advantage! Home Office PC features innovative and advanced hardware and software tailored to meet the varying needs of consumers. These consumer PC technological advances make AST's Home Office PC one of the most complete lifestyle solutions available today on a consumer system.

Full-Page Personal Document Scanner: Adding to the ideal home-office productivity solution, the Advantage! 9310 home office PC features a full-page personal document scanner that reads documents into the computer for editing, faxing and filing. Now instead of typing in information from a document, a user can simply feed the page through the scanner, have it appear on the screen, edit the content and save it in the appropriate software application. A complete scanning software package is also included.

Infrared Data Communications Port: Recognizing the increasingly mobile society in which we now live, an Infrared Data Communications Port has been included in AST's Home Office PC. An infrared data communications port allows for the wireless infrared data transfer of files between a desktop PC and a mobile computer, thereby dramatically decreasing the amount of time needed to complete this task. So a user getting ready to jump on a plane can quickly copy documents from a desktop PC to a notebook computer by simply placing the corresponding infrared ports next to each other and pushing a button.

33.6 Kbps Data/Fax/Voice Modem: Searching the Internet for up-to-the-minute financial and business information is a breeze with a 33.6 Kbps Data/Fax/Voice modem on the Advantage! 9310. Recognizing the importance of being able to access and communicate data quickly to clients across town, AST has designed its Home Office system to make operating a business from home easier than ever before.

Competitively priced at $2,499 and powered by an Intel Pentium 166MHz processor, the Advantage! Home Office system features a 2.5GB hard drive, 24MB RAM, 256KB pipeline-burst cache, 33.6 Kbps modem, 8-speed CD-ROM drive and hardware wavetable. Complimented by a whole host of software providing legal advice and information, mapping software, business finances, business graphics and desktop publishing, the Advantage! 9310 is a robust, full-featured Home Office system.
