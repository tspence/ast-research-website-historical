Vendor Product Description:

The Tecmar Technologies TS3900i is a DDS-3 tape drive has a 3.5" form factor and embedded SCSI-2 controller. 24 Gb (assuming 2:1 data compression) or 12 Gb capacity (compression disabled) per 125m DDS-3 cartridge. The increased linear density combines with new LSI's and precise control system to deliver a sustained data transfer rate of 2.4 Mb per/s. (assuming 2:1 compression). It provides a capacity to transfer rate ratio of 10,000:1, making it possible to meet the requirements of MIS managers for a shorter backup time.

Test Results:

The Tecmar TS3900i tape drive has been tested under has been tested under NT 4.0 and Netware 4.11. NT 4.0 and IntranetWare 4.11 were both tested on the Manhattan D & Manhattan S Series. Tests run were as follows:

1. Full Backup, selective restore.
2. Scheduled backup.
3. Immediate backup.

Configuration Note:

Cheyenne Arcserve for NT and Netware was able to detect the drive, see the tape, erase and format but was unable to perform a scheduled or immediate backup on the S6200. The following errors were displayed every time: 1) E6300 Window NT SCSI port error [ABSL:0240 CMD:1 1h NT:45D]. 2) E3855 Unable position tape (Tape=Test1, EC=Hardware Error).
