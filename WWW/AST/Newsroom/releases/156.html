IRVINE, Calif., Sept. 22, 1997 -- AST® Computer has made life much easier for commercial desktop users looking for state-of-the-art systems that are designed for long term ease-of-use and hassle-free maintenance. Focused on providing total computing solutions, the company today announced the addition of two new models to its Bravo MS line of commercial desktops, once more raising the bar for high-performance business computing.

The new desktop systems – Bravo MS 6266 and 6300 – are equipped with the latest and greatest technology, such as the Intel® Pentium® II processor and 440LX AGPset to handle today’s most demanding graphics applications with plenty of headroom for the future to increase the product lifecycle. A snap of a button makes upgrades and maintenance a breeze with AST’s FlexChassis. This ergonomic design reduces the downtime needed to upgrade or service the system, lowering the total cost of ownership. In addition, AST’s exclusive System Noise Reduction (SNR) technology makes the Bravo MS desktops significantly quieter than standard business PCs, improving the workplace environment by minimizing white noise pollution.

"AST responds to today’s concern of computing obsolescence with the introduction of the Bravo MS 6266 and 6300 line," explained Laura Raybin, AST’s director, brand marketing. "These innovative desktops offer more than the latest technology at a good price. They are packed with features that enhance productivity and reduce the ongoing investment in IS service and support -- a total computing solution for high-performance users."

AST stands committed to providing integrated computing systems with outstanding performance, extended life cycles and long term support. The systems’ ease-of-use clearly sets it apart from other desktop PCs utilizing the new Intel Pentium II processor and 440LX AGPset.

"Pentium II processor-based systems with the 440LX AGPset herald a new level of overall system performance and will lead to next-generation graphics," said Jag Bolaria, marketing director for Intel’s Platform Component Division. "We are pleased to see leading edge system manufacturers embracing this innovative technology."

Power + Performance = Greater Productivity and Peace of Mind

The Bravo MS family has been designed to provide worry-free ease-of-use without compromising performance, allowing users to concentrate on running their business, not their PCs. Featuring the latest in computing technology with the Intel Pentium II processor, AST’s Bravo MS systems have the power and performance necessary to run high-end applications. Intel’s MMX™ media technology and double the L1 cache (32KB) provides corporations the power they need to run today’s advanced graphics, such as intensive CAD/CAM applications, and effectively conduct video conferencing.

To boost user productivity, the Bravo MS family features the latest in core logic technology -- the Intel 440LX AGPset -- improving overall system performance and increasing graphics bandwidth. The 440LX enables the use of the Accelerated Graphics Port (AGP) allowing a transfer rate of up to 133MHz, an advantage that can mean an increase of up to four times the graphics bandwidth.

Mission-Critical Service and Support

For many corporate users, computer performance is "mission critical" to the success of their business. Recognizing this, AST offers a four-hour on-site service program for its Bravo MS and LC desktops. The Guardian CareSM program is designed to provide a "real time" solution to any computing problem. Specially trained Guardian Care technicians are prepared to arrive at business sites in the contiguous U.S. within four hours of being dispatched, 24 hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year. Additionally, advanced technical resources are available via the phone or Internet.

 

Desktop Manageability

In addition to making its systems easier to operate, AST is helping users to minimize downtime and the cost of IS support. The Bravo MS desktops are equipped with AST CommandCenter™ -- a brand-name suite of comprehensive, desktop management software utilities -- making management of data and systems faster, more logical and more cost-effective. Using the latest software solutions from industry leaders, such as Intel’s LANDesk™ Client Manager 3.0, McAfee® VirusScan™ and McAfee® WebScan™, this software combination optimizes productivity and minimizes application waste.

Pricing and Availability

With estimated street prices starting at $2,540, the Bravo MS family offers one of the best combinations of price and performance available. The newest models are currently available through AST-authorized resellers.
