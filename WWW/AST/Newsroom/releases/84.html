AST Teams With Leading Software Providers to Deliver Industry's First Turn-key Internet Server Solution

Soon-to-be-announced AST turn-key Internet server will include a full suite of Internet and Intranet software

IRVINE, CALIF, June 11, 1996 -- AST Computer today announced agreements with Software.com, Inc. and The Internet Factory, Inc., to integrate the companies' marquee products into AST's turn-key Internet server, the Manhattan™ Commerce PRO™. This is the first in a series of agreements to be put in place to bring to market the Manhattan Commerce PRO, which will be introduced by AST's Advanced Systems Group June 17 at PC Expo '96 in New York.

An Internet server like no other, the Manhattan Commerce PRO will include the extra-powerful hardware and software needed for Internet and Intranet applications. "The collaboration between AST, Software.com and The Internet Factory will offer companies an easy and cost-effective way to develop and maintain an Internet presence," said Chris Norman, Advanced Systems Group marketing director, AST. "AST chose to team with these companies not only because their innovative products fulfill important market demands, but also because both are committed to consistently being technology leaders."

AST and Software.com to Deliver Complete Electronic Messaging
Because today's business computer users depend on their electronic messaging systems as a primary means of communication, AST chose to integrate the most complete messaging server software platform available on the market: Post.Office. By including Software.com's Post.Office, the Manhattan Commerce PRO will provide Internet users with an easy-to-use, secure Internet server with the highest level of messaging capabilities and Internet protocol adherence.

The Manhattan Commerce PRO will offer a complete e-mail package through Post.Office. Post.Office provides the industry's best cross-platform, interoperable messaging solution by using open standards based on Internet protocols. In doing so, Post.Office avoids slow and cumbersome proprietary gateways to achieve higher performance and full multimedia message fidelity across the Internet.

"AST is the first hardware manufacturer to understand the messaging needs of its customers as the world embraces the Internet," said Dennis J. Cagan, executive vice president of sales, Software.com. "Internet messaging is the medium through which business communications will be done on a global scale. With Post.Office, AST customers will have a reliable, proven, secure and feature-rich turn-key offering that will provide the most complete Internet messaging server available on the market today."

Post.Office meets the ease-of-use, remote administration, high-performance and security needs of e-mail users and administrators at small, medium and large companies alike. By providing the highest level of security for Internet servers, system administrators can be assured that their Internet server will be virtually impossible to infiltrate.

AST and The Internet Factory Enable Electronic Commerce
The Internet Factory's Web server/electronic commerce bundle, Merchant Builder, will enable rapid set-up of an electronic "storefront" with AST's Manhattan Commerce PRO. Merchant Builder allows companies to completely develop a Web site to sell products to customers over the Internet, from providing initial product information to securely accepting credit card orders and managing post-sale delivery information with Internet links to major shippers such as Federal Express and UPS.

"AST sees that businesses of all sizes are selling products over the Internet," said Ernest Priestly, CEO of The Internet Factory. "Using The Internet Factory's secure and easy-to-use Merchant Builder software on AST's powerful Manhattan Commerce PRO, businesses can begin servicing their customers and handling electronic transactions on-line almost immediately."

Conducting electronic business on the Internet is easy to set up, track and maintain with Merchant Builder's unique development and maintenance features. The "Catalog Builder" feature is a template that allows businesses to input their product pictures and descriptions, pricing, delivery options and tax rates. The remaining "back-office" infrastructure for administration and maintenance is in place for the business owner to be "on-line" and in business in just a few short hours. By including a "ready-to-run" electronic store, AST's Manhattan Commerce PRO can save business owners thousands of dollars in custom programming and ongoing support costs. Merchant Builder also provides remote administration, so that a business owner can manage a remotely located Manhattan Commerce PRO.

One of the biggest concerns of individuals buying products over the Internet is security. Merchant Builder eliminates this concern by allowing encryption of all customer credit card and personal information using industry standard RSA Secure Sockets Layer protocol (SSL). Similarly, business owners can protect against unauthorized access to administrative functions by the program's use of SSL encryption plus passwords for various levels of remote administration.
