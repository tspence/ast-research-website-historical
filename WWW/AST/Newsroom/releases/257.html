AST RESEARCH NAMES TOM SCOTT TO LEAD

WORLDWIDE SALES AND MARKETING EFFORTS

 IRVINE, Calif., Oct. 21, 1997 – AST Research, Inc. today announced that Tom Scott will join the company as senior vice president, worldwide marketing and sales, effective Nov. 10.

In this newly created position, Scott, 51, will be responsible for the company’s global demand and revenue generation efforts, which include the Americas, Europe and Asia Pacific regions. He will lead AST’s marketing and sales efforts in the Americas, and will oversee marketing and sales in Europe and the Asia Pacific region. Scott will report directly to S.T. Kim, AST president and chief executive officer.

Prior to joining AST, Scott served for seven years at the Computer Systems Division of Toshiba America Information Systems, Inc. sequentially as vice president, sales, and as general manager, where he was responsible for sales, service, marketing and operations. His efforts and leadership in these two positions were responsible for helping to establish the company’s No. 1 position in notebook PC unit shipments within the U.S.

Prior to Toshiba, Scott served in various senior-level positions at AST, including vice president, U.S. sales, where he was instrumental in helping the company achieve record revenue growth rates and a reputation as one of the most dedicated indirect channel advocates.

"Tom has demonstrated considerable success during his career in meeting customer needs and creating a healthy brand image," said Mr. Kim. "These skills are key cornerstones upon which AST will leverage our resources to achieve complete customer satisfaction with our current and future product offerings."

Scott’s success in the PC industry is attributable to a simple business philosophy that includes delivering the first, fast and finest technologies; earning customer loyalty throughout the product ownership cycle; and consistently surpassing customer expectations.

"By developing high-quality business solutions that are backed by several industry-best service and support programs for both customers and resellers, AST has laid the requisite groundwork to achieve future success," said Scott. "My task will be to enhance these efforts on a global basis in order to further strengthen the reputation for the AST brand."

AST Computer develops and markets a broad spectrum of desktop, mobile and server PC products that are sold in more than 100 countries worldwide. AST systems meet a wide range of business needs, from traditional corporate business applications to customized small business programs.
