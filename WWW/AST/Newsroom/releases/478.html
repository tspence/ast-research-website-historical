Editor’s Summary:

  • With the introduction of its entry-level AST Premium® GS Server, AST® Computer has rounded-out its complete line of server solutions, including the Premium KS high-end and the Premium HS mid-range server.
  • The Premium GS server has a number of standard features and functions traditionally not found in the entry-level segment. It is equipped with either a 233MHz or 266MHz Intel Pentium® II processor and can be upgraded with a second CPU, making it an ideal entry-level platform for small to mid-sized businesses.
  • The Premium GS is currently available and can be purchased through AST-authorized resellers worldwide with an estimated street price beginning at $3,583.

 

IRVINE, Calif., Nov. 17, 1997 – Focused on delivering value-added computing solutions for small to mid-sized businesses, AST® Computer today announced an entry-level file server – the AST Premium® GS Server – to compliment its flagship mid-range product, the Premium HS. The Premium GS, offering power and performance at an economical price, has been launched concurrently with AST’s enterprise-level Quad server, the Premium KS.

"The Premium GS offers a number of standard features typically found only on mid-range servers, making it an exceptional performer for the price," said Laura Raybin, AST’s director, Brand Marketing. "We anticipate that our target market of small to mid-sized businesses will embrace the opportunity to get so many features at an entry-level price."

Offering such features and functionality is part of AST’s overall server strategy – delivering more value for the dollar by driving traditionally higher-end features into standard configurations for lower priced segments. For example, the scaleable Premium GS is equipped to support Dual 233MHz or 266MHz Intel Pentium® II processors which, along with Intel’s latest 440LX core logic chipset, significantly increases the system’s overall performance.

The server is network- and SCSI-ready right out of the box, with a 40MB/sec UltraWide SCSI controller and a 10/100BaseT Ethernet controller that are integrated to free up valuable PCI (four) and ISA (one) slots. Six hard drive bays provide up to 24GB of data capacity, and a 24X CD-ROM also comes standard. In addition, two 5.25" external drive bays provide expansion for tape backup and an additional CD-ROM.

Dual redundant cooling fans ensure ample airflow. However, to maintain high availability, the server is designed so that it can operate on a single fan, even in fully populated enclosures.

To help businesses better manage their technology assets, the Premium GS is DMI-compliant (Desktop Management Interface) and comes equipped with Intel’s LANDesk® Management software. Connectors are available to add Intel’s LANDesk Server Manager hardware for more comprehensive systems management. In addition, the Premium GS significantly simplifies network administration and reduces potential downtime by detecting many functional variations and anomalies, and alerting network management before they become critical.

Innovative Customer Support

AST stands behind its servers with a standard three-year limited warranty that includes next-business-day on-site response and a dedicated, toll-free server "hot-line" available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. In addition, AST offers Guardian CareSM, an optional, highly responsive support service that includes a four-hour on-site response from engineers specially trained by AST. Finally, to convey a complete commitment to customers’ needs, staff from AST’s service-oriented sales, marketing and technical groups are available for customer comments and questions – a true advantage over the competition.

Several other support options are available to companies who turn to AST for computing solutions. This includes AST’s web site, which offers Web Support via email as well as answers to common technical questions, software and technical bulletins and an index of files and drivers that can be easily downloaded. The company also offers support through AST Online!, its online bulletin board, which is staffed 24 hours a day, seven days a week, and its telephone bulletin board at (817) 230-6850.
