    Virtual LoJack: Absolute Computrace is computer tracing software that recovers stolen PCs. Unlike conventional security products, CompuTrace is virtually undetectable, provides a location history, and facilitates the recovery of PCs.

    CompuTrace is the revolutionary computer tracking system that locates and assists in the recovery of lost and stolen laptops. It silently and periodically calls the CompuTrace Monitoring Center. When a PC is reported stolen, CompuTrace waits for a phone line to be connected to the PC (e:mail, fax, or Internet access). It then silently dials out, informs the CompuTrace Monitoring Center of who it is and where it is calling from (via Caller ID). If the computer is not connected to the modem, the software will check every few seconds until the modem is available. The CompuTrace Monitoring Center initiates the CompuTrace Theft Recovery Procedure to locate and recover the stolen PC. CompuTrace is intended to work from any phone line in the United States or Canada, even from an unlisted line or one that is using Caller ID blocking.
