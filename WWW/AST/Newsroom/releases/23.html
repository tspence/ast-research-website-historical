IRVINE, Calif., March 17, 1997 -- AST® Computer (NASDAQ:ASTA) raises the bar on server power and functionality as the company today introduces the next generation of its Manhattan S6200 and Manhattan Commerce Pro servers. Enhanced with the industry's fastest and most versatile RAID controller, high-speed Ultra/Wide SCSI and an enormous 54GB of storage, the new Manhattan servers respond to business customers' needs for robust and comprehensive solutions.

"Small- and medium-sized businesses are looking for a server that provides them with leading technology accompanied by individually focused customer service and support programs," said Laura Raybin, director, brand marketing for AST. "We've gone the extra mile for our business customers with unique services like computer-based training and effortless Internet connectivity. These versatile and easy-to-use server solutions allow businesses to concentrate on doing business, without wasting time and energy over server installation and maintenance."

Manhattan S6200 Flexes Its Hardware Muscle

Building upon an already solid hardware platform, the enhanced Manhattan S6200 Series offers advanced new features that maximize server performance. The S6200 Series is equipped with AMI's three channel MegaRAID Ultra PCI SCSI Disk Array Controller, the fastest and most versatile RAID controller available. Offering extensive software support, the MegaRAID-equipped S6200 Series allows users to add new hard drives without bringing down the system and conduct online RAID volume expansion and conversion with ease.

The Manhattan S6200 Series offers Ultra/Wide SCSI, doubling access speed to 40MB/sec for fast data retrieval. Rounding out the new feature set, the S6200 powerhouse boosts productivity with up to 1GB of ECC RAM, 256KB or 512KB of L2 cache, and massive storage capabilities with up to six 9GB hot swap drive bays for a total of 54GB.

The Manhattan S6200 Series also provides a fast 200MHz Intel Pentium® Pro processor with 512KB cache and is configured to handle dual-processor capabilities. Offering exceptional manageability, the S6200 is equipped with Intel's LANDesk® Server Management software. Meeting users' needs for expansion, the S6200 offers five PCI slots, two EISA slots and one shared PCI/EISA slot. For users who are migrating to 32-bit operating environments, some models come pre-installed with Windows NT® Server 4.0. A reliable solution, the S6200 provides unattended system recovery and is designed, tested and certified to optimize Novell® NetWare® and IntranetWare and Microsoft® Windows NT server applications.

Manhattan Commerce Pro - A Turnkey Internet Solution

The enhanced Manhattan Commerce Pro adds new levels of performance and functionality to its acclaimed predecessor, recognized by PC/Computing Magazine as a 1996 MVP award finalist for the best Internet server. Building on the Manhattan S6200's enhanced hardware platform with a comprehensive suite of Internet/Intranet software, the Manhattan Commerce Pro is the ideal Internet solution for small- and medium-sized businesses.

The Manhattan Commerce Pro system's complete software package provides impressive features not available in other servers, making customer and employee communication a breeze. For instance, users can set up email internally or externally over the Internet for fast and efficient communication worldwide. Those who need assistance developing and editing their own Web site will benefit from the system's handy database templates. Users can also easily establish chat rooms and news groups to obtain customer opinions, gather information on specific topics and set up internal discussion groups. In addition, the Manhattan Commerce Pro helps users sell their products and services over the Internet, with convenient catalogue templates and airtight security for transactions.

One of the most easy-to-use solutions available, the Manhattan Commerce Pro is up and running in a snap with pre-installed Microsoft Windows NT Server 4.0. Users receive instant access to the Internet through AST's unique relationship with Netcom, a national Internet service provider. Pushing the envelope to help users save time and money, AST also equipped the Manhattan Commerce Pro with user-friendly training modules from CBT, the leader in computer based training.

Pricing, Availability and Warranty

The Manhattan S6200 Series and Manhattan Commerce Pro are scheduled to be available in April, 1997. Estimated street prices start at $6,496 for the S6200 Series and $8,451 for the Manhattan Commerce Pro.

Both Manhattan server products carry a three-year on-site limited warranty covering hardware components. An upgraded limited warranty providing four-hour response time is available through the GuardianCareSM program available in the United States.
