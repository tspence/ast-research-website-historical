IRVINE, Calif., Nov. 17, 1997 – With the introduction of its new AST Premium® KS Server today, AST® Computer now offers a high-end server solution to meet the needs of the business market, complementing its flagship Premium HS mid-range server and the concurrently introduced Premium GS entry-level server. The new Premium KS features a four-way symmetric multiprocessor (SMP) capability, delivering enterprise-level server performance for businesses that require ultra-high performance and reliability.

"The Premium KS has the power and performance to run a business," said Laura Raybin, director, Brand Marketing, for AST. "With its multiple redundancies and built-in fault tolerance, the Premium KS will help end-users focus on running their businesses, not their networks."

Low-Risk/High-Availability

The Premium KS offers a combination of features that make it a low-risk solution for the enterprise market. It is designed to deliver high availability and manageability that can reduce downtime and service costs. In addition, it employs a field-proven Intel main board design that has been certified under today’s top network operating systems, so end-users and resellers can be confident that their applications will operate properly.

The Premium KS features redundant fans and power supplies to ensure high availability along with built-in management circuitry that monitors the entire system for signs of trouble. To minimize data loss, the system can also be configured to send SNMP trap information in the event of trouble. Using Intel’s LANDesk® Server Manager, IS managers can trouble-shoot the system remotely, which simplifies service and reduces support costs. In addition, features such as the mechanical lock loop, covering both front and back doors; electronic lock-out of the power, reset, and keyboard; chassis intrusion switch; and other component-level intrusion detection devices help ensure the data security and availability required from an enterprise-level server.

Unrivaled Performance

The Premium KS uses Intel’s 450GX core logic and comes with two 200MHz Intel Pentium® Pro processors. The system can be rapidly and easily expanded to four CPUs for symmetric multiprocessing (SMP). To enhance performance, scalability is another feature of the Premium KS, with 12 hot-pluggable SCSI hard drive bays so users can upgrade or change drives without downtime. The base configuration has two 4GB hard drives with 10 additional drive bays for expansion, and 128MB of memory upgradeable to 4GB. The system includes four 5.5" peripheral bays, one of which can hold a 16X CD-ROM.

Other features include a high-speed intelligent network interface card (NIC), a Mylex three-channel UltraWide SCSI RAID controller, and two integrated Fast/Wide SCSI channels. While such features are typically offered as options on competitive systems, they are standard on the Premium KS and help reduce reseller and end-user integration time and costs.

Up to 2GB of memory can be placed on the memory card to further increase performance and an additional 4GB memory module can be added to meet the needs of virtually any current application.

Innovative Customer Support

AST stands behind its servers with a standard three-year limited warranty that includes next-business-day on-site response and a dedicated, toll-free server "hot-line" available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. In addition, AST offers Guardian CareSM, an optional, highly responsive support service that includes a four-hour on-site response from engineers specially trained by AST. Finally, to convey a complete commitment to customers’ needs, staff from AST’s service-oriented sales, marketing and technical groups are available for customer comments and questions – a true advantage over the competition.

Several other support options are available to companies who turn to AST for computing solutions. This includes AST’s web site, which offers Web Support via email as well as answers to common technical questions, software and technical bulletins and an index of files and drivers that can be easily downloaded. The company also offers support through AST Online!, its online bulletin board, which is staffed 24 hours a day, seven days a week, and its telephone bulletin board at (817) 230-6850.
