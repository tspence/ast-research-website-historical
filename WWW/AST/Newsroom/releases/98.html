AST RESEARCH NAMES MICHAEL WILLCOCKS TO LEAD ASIA PACIFIC REGION

IRVINE, Calif., February 29, 1996 -- AST Research Inc. (ASTA-NASDAQ) today announced Michael Willcocks will join the company as senior vice president of the company's Asia Pacific region and an officer.

Willcocks, 47, who formerly served at Apple Computer and Wang Laboratories in executive-level worldwide sales and marketing positions, will be responsible for all sales, marketing and service and support activities in the Asia Pacific region. He will report to Ian Diery, AST president and chief executive officer.

"Michael will become a significant asset in helping grow our Asia Pacific revenues and establishing AST as the company-of-choice to supply leading-edge products worldwide," said Diery." His extensive experience with major computer companies, specifically in the international arena, will greatly complement and strengthen our approach to achieving AST's turnaround goals. His successful track record of addressing sales and marketing issues globally will significantly boost AST's efforts in increasing our regional and worldwide presence."

Willcock's charter is to develop the enormous potential within the Asia Pacific region, which includes China, Hong Kong, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Taiwan, Japan, Pakistan, India and the Middle Eastern countries. In addition, he will further the company's revenue growth efforts in AST's Asian Distributor Organization (ADO) countries such as Thailand and Vietnam, while leveraging AST's No. 1 market position in China into other territories.

A 25-year high-technology industry veteran, Willcocks has extensive global experience, most lately at Apple Computer, where he served as vice president, worldwide enterprise and government marketing, and vice president, global accounts. Prior to Apple, he held positions at Interleaf Inc. as general manager, Northern Europe operations and managing director, Interleaf UK. At Wang Laboratories, he served in various positions, including director, worldwide operations.

"Based upon an assessment of the company's core competencies and strategic direction, I am very excited to join AST's senior management team," said Willcocks.

"I am particularly impressed with the depth of the team's resources, their global focus on customer satisfaction, as well as their single-minded determination to return AST to a market share growth position as the premier supplier of leading edge products worldwide."

Corporate Background
AST Research Inc., a member of the Fortune 500 list of America's largest industrial and service companies, is one of the world's leading personal computer manufacturers. The $2.468 billion company develops a broad spectrum of desktop, mobile and server PC products that are sold in more than 100 countries worldwide.
