AST® COMPUTER DECLARES WAR ON PC INFECTION BY ARMING ITS NEW BRAVO COMPUTERS WITH McAFEE'S INDUSTRY-LEADING SOFTWARE

AST Responds to Need for Superior Virus Protection By Preloading Its Latest
Commercial Desktop Computers With McAfee VirusScan and WebScan

IRVINE, Calif., May 27, 1997 - Striking a strategic blow against computer viruses, AST® Computer announced today that its Bravo LC and Bravo MS commercial desktops and minitowers will offer McAfee's VirusScan and WebScan anti-virus software. These two software packages provide Bravo users with powerful armor against more than 11,000 different computer viruses at all points of entry.

"Bravo corporate customers depend on AST to provide computing solutions that boost office productivity and offer outstanding reliability," stated Laura Raybin, director, brand marketing, AST Computer. "By equipping our Bravo desktops with one of the most highly regarded and proven virus protection packages available, we're giving our customers a strong defense against computer damage and downtime."

VirusScan 3.0 - The World's Leading Virus Protection

With a virus detection rate of 98 percent proven in several independent studies, McAfee VirusScan finds and removes nearly every virus that a Bravo PC user might encounter. It provides unparalleled protection against Microsoft® Word and Excel macros, boot sector infectors, file infectors, encrypted viruses, multi-partite viruses, polymorphic viruses and new viruses written in Microsoft Visual Basic®. With a worldwide unit marketshare of 52 percent, McAfee VirusScan is the world's most popular anti-virus software, selling more units that all other packages combined.

"We applaud AST for taking proactive and decisive steps to protect their Bravo customers from viruses," said Peter Watkins, vice president and general manager, Security Products Division, McAfee. "By bundling McAfee's anti-virus protection with AST's industry-leading software package, AST customers have the tools they need to work smarter, faster and safer."

WebScan Protects Internet Travelers

McAfee WebScan provides Bravo users with an additional layer of protection against one of the most common sources of system infection -- the Internet. The McAfee software guards users by detecting viruses on Web Browser downloads and automatically scanning e-mail attachments before they are opened. WebScan easily integrates with all major browsers including Netscape Navigator, Internet Explorer, Mosaic and NetCruiser. Offering secure protection to Bravo customers, WebScan allows users to safely download files, send and receive e-mail and surf more than 100,000 Web sites on-line.

Availability

McAfee VirusScan and WebScan are scheduled to be available on Bravo LC and Bravo MS commercial desktop computers mid-May. WebScan is only available on systems offering Windows® 95.
