AST® COMPUTER TAKES THE COMDEX EXPERIENCE OUT OF LAS VEGAS AND LIVE ONTO THE WEB

Special ASTVnet Web Site Allows "Surfers" Worldwide to See and Hear All That COMDEX Has To Offer - Directly From The AST Booth

LAS VEGAS, Nov. 14, 1996 - AST Computer (NASDAQ: ASTA), a leader in new technology implementation, innovatively brings the sights and sounds of COMDEX '96 right to your PC. Using the new Manhattan™ Presence Pro Internet server, AST is transmitting a live broadcast feed from AST's booth directly to the company's ASTVnet web site. AST has commissioned InterNT to deliver a "live Webcast" of COMDEX, allowing those with Internet access the ability to experience COMDEX from anywhere in the world. Providing minute-by-minute action, AST's Website is easily accessed at http://www.ast.com continuously throughout the show, Nov. 18 - 22.

"By combining AST's state-of-the-art hardware with today's advanced technologies, AST is able to provide the powerful solutions to make this unique interactive experience possible," said Dan Sheppard, vice president of global corporate marketing at AST. "AST took the lead by introducing this technology application at COMDEX/Canada last July. Again, we're expecting thousands to log on, and for many, experience live audio and video for the first time."

Close Encounters
Powered by the Manhattan Presence Pro, AST's new turn-key Internet server, ASTVnet will feature three cameras and a live audio feed, via the Web, straight to the comfort of your own PC. The site will feature digital images from the show floor that will be consistently refreshed, live video of stage events and entertainment in the AST booth and continuous audio from the show floor.

To provide a complete interactive experience, an on-line contest will be held to give away an award-winning AST Ascentia J notebook computer. ASTVnet will also include a COMDEX show schedule as a guide to the more than 2,100 exhibitors displaying the latest technologies at the show.

Manhattan Presence Pro
Debuting at COMDEX, the Manhattan Presence Pro offers Intel® Pentium® Pro processor power at a desktop computer price. Beginning at an estimated street price under $6,000, first-time server sites to large offices will benefit from the outstanding price and performance offered in this complete Internet/Intranet server solution. The Manhattan Presence Pro includes pre-loaded Web server software, Web authoring, E-mail, training software and search engines - all the elements needed to get a business up-and-running on the World Wide Web in a matter of hours.

Don't Get Loaded Down - Download
For anyone who logs on to the AST Comdex Home Page, they will not only see the sights and sounds of the show, but will also be able to download information on AST's award-winning product line. Linking directly back to AST's Home Page, product specifications and features are available for AST's Ascentia™ notebook computers, Bravo commercial desktops, Advantage! ® consumer desktops and Manhattan servers.
