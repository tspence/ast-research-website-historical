The AST Premium GS server platform brings the power of the Intel® Pentium® II processor to the entry-level server marketplace. With up to two Pentium II processors, plus the new Intel 440LX chipset, the Premium GS server continues the drive to bring cost-sensitive, entry-level customers the power and capability previously seen only in more expensive products. The Premium GS server is available initially with either a 233 MHz or 266 MHz Pentium II processor with 512K L2 cache. The 266 MHz Pentium II processor delivers double the performance of previous generation entry level offerings and is designed to accept future versions of the Pentium II processor.

Key Sales and Marketing Messages

Unrivaled Server Feature Set

The Premium GS server is designed to keep you on top in the cost-driven, entry-level server marketplace. A complete set of on-board controllers for network, disk I/O and graphics help reduce your time to market while increasing the reliability and cost-effectiveness of your finished product. The baseboard incorporates the latest PCI I/O technology, including built-in support for 10/100 Mb Ethernet, 40 MB/sec Ultra-Wide SCSI and SVGA graphics.

Extensive configurability gives you plenty of options for tailoring systems to meet a wide range of customer requirements, and enables your customers to expand their servers as their changing business needs dictate. Storage capability includes six 1" high 3.5" hard drives or three 1.6" hard drives, as well as a 24X CD-ROM drive and 1.44 MB floppy drive. Two additional 5.25" bays are available for removable media drives. Five open slots let you configure a full complement of four PCI add-ins and offer support for a legacy ISA add-in card.

Purpose-Built Workhorse!

Information is the lifeblood of today's businesses, and when servers go down, the flow of information is slowed or even halted. As a purpose-built server, the Premium GS goes beyond desktop-level manageability and security with features designed to keep vital servers running efficiently 24 hours a day. The board and chassis are fully instrumented to communicate via the industry-standard Desktop Management Interface (DMI) protocol, making a wealth of data available to leading system-wide management and security applications. To fully utilize all of its system management capabilities, the Premium GS is shipped with award-winning Intel LANDesk® Server Manager software. This software provides both in-band and out-of-band monitoring capability, allowing LAN managers to reduce support costs and increase system uptime by monitoring, diagnosing and controlling the server remotely. An optional server-monitoring module adds emergency management capabilities over telephone lines and is accessible even if the server is powered down.

The Premium GS is targeted at VARs and end users at the entry-level end of the price curve. More than half of the servers sold are in this price range, making the GS an extremely attractive and exciting low-end offer from AST. Entry server buyers are looking for basic server features (server-performance NICs, Ultra/Wide SCSI, large disk capacity), and server-type product support, and are not usually looking for ultra-high speed of fault-tolerance. The GS provides an extremely effective balance between basic server features and aggressive pricing.

Top Differentiators

Price

The Premium GS is aggressively priced slightly under similarly configured Compaq®, IBM®, and HP® systems. If price is the motivating factor, AST has a system for them!

Performance

The AST Premium GS includes many standard features that the others need to add to the base price. Although the base unit costs of other entry-level servers are lower, they end up costing more once necessary networking components are added. Adding these features standard significantly reduces integration mistakes and compatibility issues.

Announce Strategy

The Premium GS will be announced on November 3, with the high-end Premium KS. They will complete AST's server product line, adding to our flagship product, the Premium HS. The completion of the product line with high-quality, easy-to-sell servers will simplify your efforts to satisfy the demands of the end user and VAR who require a server migration strategy and a well-round, single-vendor server solution.
