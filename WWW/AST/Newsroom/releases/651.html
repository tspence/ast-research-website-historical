Vendor Product Description:

The Tecmar Technologies TS3800i is a DDS-2 tape drive has a 3.5" form factor and embedded SCSI-2 controller. Storage capacity up to 8Gb on one data cartridge with a MTBF of 200,000 power-on hours at 20% duty cycle. The drive has a native (uncompressed) data transfer rate of 778 kilobytes per second, up to 1.5 Mb per second with industry standard DDS-2 DCLZ data compression enabled.

Test Results:

The Tecmar TS3800i tape drive has been tested under has been tested under NT 4.0 and Netware 4.11. NT 4.0 and IntranetWare 4.11 were both tested on the Manhattan D, Manhattan S, Premium GS, Premium HS & Premium KS Series. Tests run were as follows:

1. Full Backup, selective restore.
2. Scheduled backup.
3. Immediate backup.

Configuration Note:

Cheyenne Arcserve for NT and Netware was able to detect the drive, see the tape, erase and format but was unable to perform a scheduled or immediate backup on the S6200. The following errors were displayed every time: 1) E6300 Window NT SCSI port error [ABSL:0240 CMD:1 1h NT:45D]. 2) E3855 Unable position tape (Tape=Test1, EC=Hardware Error).
