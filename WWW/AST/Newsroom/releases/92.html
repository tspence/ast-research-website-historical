AST CHALKS UP ANOTHER FIRST WITH LEADING-EDGE
CD-ROM MINICHANGER MANAGEMENT SOFTWARE ON ADVANTAGE! MULTIMEDIA PCs

AST Is First-to-Market In Indirect Channel with 6X CD-ROM Minichanger Using
Technology From Smart Storage

Editorial Summary:

  • AST is first-to-market with 6X CD-ROM drives with three CD-minichangers for the indirect channel, using the latest in minichanger management software from Smart Storage.
  • Bundled on its high-end Advantage! multimedia systems, AST once more demonstrates its quick-to-market strategy by providing consumers leading-edge technology at the right time.

IRVINE, Calif., Feb. 20, 1996 -- AST Computer today delivered another home run by being the first PC manufacturer to introduce to the indirect channel a 6X CD-ROM with three-CD minichangers for its home and home office users.

Shipping on AST's new line of Intel Pentium® processor-based Advantage! PCs, the 6X CD-ROM drives use designated SmartCD Manager™ software, from Smart Storage, located in Andover, Mass. Smart Storage is a recognized market leader in network-based CD-Recordable (CD-R) management software.

"AST has partnered with Smart Storage because of its strong reputation and demonstrated expertise with CD jukebox management software. Users now have an added degree of flexibility in running and managing collections of CD-ROMS and audio CDs," said AST's Dan Sheppard, director of desktop marketing.

AST's Advantage! systems incorporate minichangers provided by Tottori Sanyo Electric Company, located in Tottori City, Japan, on which the management software from Smart Storage is bundled.

"The combination of AST's technical innovation with the market leader in network-based CD-Recordable (CD-R) management software provides one of the most robust combinations of power and performance surpassing the technology dreams of today's consumer," stated Peter Smails, vice president of product marketing, of Smart Storage.

AST believes that the combination of a powerful minichanger from a well-respected company like Sanyo with the software management expertise of Smart Storage enables users to access collections of CD-ROMS on their desktop in a timely and efficient manner.

"It makes perfect sense for the consumer to upgrade a basic CD-ROM drive to a minichanger," said Sheppard.

"We expect that minichangers will become just as prevalent in the home computer market as multi-CD players are in the audio market over the next twelve months," he added.

Key Benefits
SmartCD Manager allows users to:

  • Catalog collections of reference, audio, multimedia and photo CDs for easy identification with descriptive information for each disc.
  • Easily inventory, select, load and run individual CDs with a user-friendly icon and menu driven interface.
  • Access all three CDs by a single drive letter, instead of a drive letter for each disc, allowing the CDs to be removed and inserted in different slots inside the changer without affecting playback or program operation.

Chauffeur and Driver All In One
SmartCD Manager represents a significant improvement over existing device drivers by integrating a graphical database, changer device management capabilities and a launch pad for CD-based applications into a single environment. According to Smart Storage, it is the first product of its kind to provide simple, functional tools to access up to three discs on the AST CD changer.

High Growth Market
According to Freeman and Associates Inc., a high tech market research firm in northern California, the CD is quickly becoming the data management and storage solution of choice among consumers. For worldwide shipments from all suppliers during 1994, the survey reveals that shipments of CD-ROM drives were up 148 percent over 1993 levels, with revenue up 95 percent.

"CD-ROM is a key enabling technology for multimedia and mass storage because CD technology is uniquely qualified to handle all types of digital data including text, audio and motion video," noted Sheppard.

Complete with plenty of expansion slots and up to 2.1 GB storage capacity, the latest crop of Advantage! systems are blazingly fast powerhouses for value-conscious and power-hungry consumers.
