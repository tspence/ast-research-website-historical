JUST RELEASED AST® OWNER SURVEY REVEALS THAT 97 PERCENT OF ADVANTAGE!® CUSTOMERS ARE HAPPY WITH THEIR PURCHASE

Additional Results Confirm AST's Solid Service and Support Program and an Increase in Internet Activity through Advantage! Online Service Subscriptions

IRVINE, Calif., Oct. 1, 1996 -- Reinforcing AST Computer's (NASDAQ: ASTA) commitment to customer satisfaction, the 1996 AST Advantage! Owner Survey reveals that 97 percent of Advantage! owners are happy with their desktop computer purchase and give AST a strong approval rating. The survey results also confirm that three quarters of Advantage! users who utilize technical support give AST's support group a solid recommendation. Additionally, the survey indicates a nearly 60 percent increase in online service subscriptions obtained by Advantage! PC users.

"The 1996 AST Advantage! Owner Survey clearly indicates that new owners of Advantage! desktop computers are very pleased with their purchase. The most often cited areas of approval were price, features, power/performance, software bundles, quality, ease of set-up, ease of use and especially AST's service and support," said Cory Schwartz, president of ConsumerQuest, an independent Los Angeles-based market research company that conducted the survey of over 1000 Advantage! owners. "For example, one interesting finding is that 97 percent of those who contacted AST for technical support said that they were happy with their desktop system."

Advantage! Owners are Happy Owners
The 1996 Advantage! Owners Survey indicates remarkable satisfaction ratings from current owners of Advantage! multimedia desktop computers. Of the 97 percent reporting a strong approval rating, 30 percent said "they loved it" and another 48 percent said "they really like it." These results are similar to a 1995 survey which also confirmed that Advantage! owners were pleased with the decision they had made in choosing AST. In addition, 92 percent said they would recommend AST to a friend.

"From a company perspective, we are very pleased with the results from this survey because it indicates that we are listening to our customers and sales channels to produce Advantage! computing solutions that our users require and appreciate," explained Terry Baker, director of marketing for AST's worldwide consumer products.

Stellar Service and Support
AST Advantage! technical service and support received high praise and recommendations from the survey respondents based on a number of different programs that are available to Advantage! users. This includes a toll-free hot-line for users in the United States, available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Additionally, all Advantage! PCs come equipped with AST-LifeLine™, an interactive technical support system that incorporates integrated voice and data protocol with custom AST technical support software. The survey indicated that over 50 percent of Advantage! technical support users utilize AST-LifeLine.

Other support tools available to Advantage! users include the AST InfoLINE (an automated voice response unit), an electronic bulletin board service, AST Info-FAX (a 24-hour voice-automated fax-response product information source), AST On-Line! (an extensive on-line support available 24 hours a day, seven days a week) and AST's own bulletin board service.

"AST is committed to providing customers with the highest level of support and service, which is why we emphasize the development of new programs to better meet the needs of our customers," said Matt Doyle, AST's vice president of service and support for the Americas. "For example, our customers wanted an interactive technical support system to provide immediate support requirements. We answered that need and are very pleased that over 50 percent of our Advantage! technical support customers are utilizing AST-LifeLine."

Getting On and Staying On-Line
In sync with the migrational trend toward the Internet, the Advantage! Owner Survey revealed that there was a 50 percent increase in online service subscriptions among Advantage! owners from 1995 to 1996. AST expected this growth last year and planned accordingly by bundling popular online software and other user-friendly features to make accessing the Internet and online services easier and fun for Advantage! users.

"Accessing and communicating via online services is a big growth area and one that AST will continue to address. The survey results reinforce that foresight, indicating that approximately 60 percent of users who purchased an Advantage! system went online within two weeks of the purchase," explained Terry Baker. "By addressing these online needs and other customer requirements, AST is clearly targeting our products accurately, which is resulting in an exceptional customer satisfaction rating."

The Advantage! Advantage
AST has moved beyond the marketing approach of providing a standard PC for all home computer needs, in favor of delivering customized systems designed to fulfill a diverse set of consumer interests and lifestyles. The new line of Advantage! consumer personal desktops are designed to meet the needs of four market segments: Home Office, Communications, Gaming and Education, as well as general consumer computing applications. The new line-up encompasses the most advanced technologies available featuring everything from interactive communications and high-resolution 3-D graphics and 3-D sound to full-page scanners and infrared remote capabilities.

The Advantage! Award Winning Track Record
AST Advantage! PC systems have received numerous awards for outstanding performance and features. Along with earning Family PC's "Recommended Buy" three times over the past year, Advantage! systems have won Home PC's "Editor's Choice Award" (April 1996) and Family PC's "Best Buy Award" (May 1996).
