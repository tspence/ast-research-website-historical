AST TEAMS WITH DISNEY INTERACTIVE FOR PREMIERE OF "THE HUNCHBACK OF NOTRE DAME" GAME

AST Advantage! Multimedia PCs Located in 150 Theaters Nationwide Previewing Latest Creation From Disney Interactive

Editorial Summary:

  • AST Computer once more demonstrates its first-to-market strategy by becoming the first PC manufacturer to preview Disney Interactive's new PC game.
  • AST Advantage! multimedia computers are currently previewing Disney Interactive's upcoming PC game, GameBreak! The Hunchback of Notre Dame Topsy Turvy Games in 150 theaters nationwide.
  • AST Computer is the exclusive provider of multimedia PCs for this first-of-its-kind, hands-on preview event.

IRVINE, Calif., July 2, 1996 -- Dancing gypsies, a bell ringer, talking gargoyles and AST Advantage! multimedia PCs appear together at a theater near you this Summer as AST Computer (ASTA-NASDAQ) teams with Disney Interactive to preview the new PC game, GameBreak! The Hunchback of Notre Dame Topsy Turvy Games.

Coinciding with the premiere of Walt Disney Picture's 34th full-length animated feature, The Hunchback of Notre Dame, AST Computer has joined forces with Disney Interactive to be the exclusive provider of multimedia PCs to preview Disney's upcoming PC game. One hundred and fifty theaters throughout the top ten markets in the country are home to AST Advantage! 828 multimedia PC's and Disney Interactive's GameBreak! The Hunchback of Notre Dame Topsy Turvy Games, which is due in stores on August 14, 1996.

"This is the first time that a PC manufacturer has teamed up with Disney Interactive to offer a hands-on sneak preview of Disney's latest software on a national scale. Millions of families will be able to experience first-hand our strong multimedia hardware coupled with Disney's graphically rich software," said Dan Sheppard, vice president of worldwide product marketing.

"We are delighted to work with AST to showcase our soon-to-be-released GameBreak! The Hunchback of Notre Dame Topsy Turvy Games," said Hope Neiman, vice president, marketing for Disney Interactive. "With AST's help, we look forward to introducing our newest game to filmgoers and Disney fans throughout the country and to creating widespread excitement about Topsy Turvy Games."

Utilizing the latest technology for the consumer market, the Advantage! 828 features an Intel Pentium 166 MHz processor, 16MB RAM, 6X CD-ROM drive with a three-disc changer, 64-bit local bus VGA graphics and 3-D sound with Wavetable synthesis.

GameBreak! The Hunchback of Notre Dame Topsy Turvy Games includes five fast-paced PC CD-ROM games set outside Paris' Notre Dame Cathedral during the annual Festival of Fools -- a day where everything is topsy-turvy. More than 8000 cells of original Disney animation were created especially for this game, offering players the opportunity to relive the fun and adventure of the film and interact with many of its characters.

At A Theater Near You
The AST sponsored preview can now be experienced in 150 theaters in the top 10 markets nationwide: Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Dallas, Los Angeles, New York, Philadelphia, Phoenix, San Francisco and Washington, D.C.

Disney Interactive Background
Disney Interactive develops, publishes and markets a wide variety of interactive family-oriented entertainment and educational materials, including CD-ROMs, video games and online products. Since its formation in December 1994, Disney Interactive has released five of the best-selling children's software titles and several chart-topping video games. Worldwide sales and distribution are through Buena Vista Home Video in North America and Buena Vista Home Entertainment in all major international territories.
