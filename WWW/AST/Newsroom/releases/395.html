Special Pricing for Warranty Upgrades

All special bids must go through the Special Pricing Group, including those required for warranty upgrades. Please follow these 3 simple steps:

  1. Use the special bid forms that have already been distributed.
  2. Add your warranty pricing requests as an extra line item on the special bid form.
  3. Send your completed request form electronically to the Special Pricing Group in Irvine.

Please do not contact service directly for any warranty special pricing bids.

VARBusiness Annual Report Card Results

VARBusiness recently released the results of their latest Annual Report Card. For the report, VARBusiness surveyed thousands of resellers on the best vendor programs, products and relationship issues.

In recent years, AST has gone from winning the Annual Report Card in 1993 to a disappointing last place finish in 1996. As you know, we have initiated a major come back effort by implementing a responsive, "easy to work with" attitude across all areas of business. Our efforts are starting to pay off!

 Our dedication to the reseller channel has ranked AST Computer Number One in "Ease of Doing Business", according to the 1997 VARBusiness Annual Report Card! There's no doubt that this is due to your hard work in improving responsiveness and to the programs implemented in the last year to help our resellers succeed.

You have to agree that this is a remarkable achievement for AST! But we can't stop here. We must continue to work on our programs and support. Our goal for 1998 should be to take first place overall in the VARBusiness Annual Report Card.

Ovation! Program Update

 We are getting closer to the launch of the new Ovation! Program. In fact, at Comdex we will feature the new program within our booth at a special "programs" kiosk. Here, resellers can review the completely re-designed Ovation! website, find out about our programs, and register on-line.

Before this Comdex launch, you will get a chance to hear all about the new program through a series of conference calls with each region. Be sure to attend the scheduled call to hear about all of the exciting new program elements for 1998.

In the next week, you will each receive your new Ovation! Program binder, detailing all of the new program elements. The binder has been designed for you to use as a presentation tool as well as a reference guide for you on the program. Keep a lookout!
