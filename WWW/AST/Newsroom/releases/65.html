NEW AST® ADVANTAGE!® MULTIMEDIA GAMER PC BRINGS THE LATEST TECHNOLOGIES TO HOME ENTERTAINMENT

3-D Sight and Sound, Logitech Wingman Extreme Joystick and Digital Simultaneous Voice Data (DSVD) Modem Advance Game Playing to a New Dimension

Editorial Summary:

  • The Advantage! Gamer PC addresses the needs of the rapidly growing gaming market by delivering the most advanced consumer PC technologies available, including high-resolution 3-D graphics and 3-D sound, 24-watt speakers and 40-watt subwoofer, the Logitech Wingman Extreme Joystick and a Digital Simultaneous Voice Data Modem.
  • Gamer software packages such as Electronic Arts Shockwave™, Interplay™ Virtual Soccer™, Interplay Whiplash™, Electronic Arts Extreme Pinball™, Psygnosis Destruction Derby™ and Formgen Terminal Velocity™ are included.
  • The competitively-priced Advantage! Gamer PCs have an estimated sales price starting at $2,699.

IRVINE, Calif., Sept. 9, 1996 -- Recognizing that gaming is one of the fastest growing markets in the personal computer industry, AST Computer (NASDAQ:ASTA) today announced the availability of the Advantage! 9315 & 9316 Gamer PCs, which are tailored to meet the demands of today's sophisticated home entertainment user. These systems are part of AST's new fall line-up of lifestyle-geared consumer PCs.

Recent findings from an AST Computer customer survey show that in 1996, there is a 28 percent increase in the number of computer users who identify gaming as the primary reason for purchasing a home computer, compared to the year before. This number exceeds findings from research firm IDC/Link Resources who expect the overall consumer PC market to grow 17 percent in 1996.

"AST understands that gaming is a serious business," said Terry Baker, director of marketing, worldwide consumer products. "We understand the unique interests and lifestyles of game players, and have tailored our Advantage! 9315 and 9316 PCs to specifically meet these needs."

Advantage! gaming systems come complete with rich multimedia content and compelling, high-resolution 3-dimensional realism by featuring standard components such as S3 ViRGE graphics and video acceleration, 3-D sound, 24-watt Altec Lansing speakers, a 40-watt subwoofer and a Logitech Wingman Extreme Joystick. Powerful 166MHz and 200MHz Pentium processors, large hard drives and 32MB RAM round out this spectacular gaming experience. Prices start at $2,699.

Experience Leading-Edge Technologies
Imagine taking a break from your spreadsheets by "clicking into" a vast 3-D world with full 3-D sound, where you can soar and explore, fly up or down, battle alien forces and return victoriously. Or race through gravity-defying racetracks with the use of a responsive joystick for absolute control.

The new Advantage! multimedia Gamer PC features innovative first-to-market hardware and software, including:

3-Dimensional Sight and Sound: Hearing the roaring sound of jet engines and seeing the fast approaching target coming straight at you ... these are crucial sensations to any PC game. AST addresses these key factors by including 3-D hardware wavetable and 3-D sound, which is enhanced by 24-watt Altec Lansing high-end speakers and a 40-watt subwoofer, as standard features on the Advantage! 9315 & 9316 Gamer models. Utilizing S3 Inc.'s ViRGE 3-D accelerators, these Gamer systems deliver high performance graphics and video capabilities for PC gaming.

Logitech Wingman Extreme Joystick: Maneuvering a race car through gravity-defying and stunt-filled race tracks takes both skill and the ultimate joystick. A standard hardware feature on the Advantage! 9315 & 9316 Gamer systems, the Logitech Wingman Extreme Joystick is ergonomically designed to allow the user a complete gaming experience.

Digital Simultaneous Voice Data (DSVD) 28.8 modem: Gaming is no longer a solitary experience but now also a competitive activity that can also involve two or more people playing one another simultaneously. The DSVD modem provides on-line real-time gaming. This allows gaming enthusiasts to compete against one another in real-time to enhance the gaming experience.

Gaming Software Package: In addition to a well-rounded general software package that includes environment, productivity, reference, fun, education and communications titles, the Advantage! 9315 and 9316 systems also come with an action-packed gaming bundle. Featuring titles such as Interplay's Virtual Soccer, Interplay Whiplash, Electronic Arts Shockwave and Extreme Pinball, Psygnosis Destruction Derby and Formgen Terminal Velocity, these powerful systems are sure to please even the most sophisticated gamer.
