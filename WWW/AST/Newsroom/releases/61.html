AST'S NEW ENTRY-LEVEL MANHATTAN™ SERVERS OFFER PENTIUM® PRO PROCESSOR POWER AT A DESKTOP COMPUTER PRICE

Beginning at Under $4,000, First-Time Server Sites to Large Offices Will Benefit From The Outstanding Price And Premium Performance Offered With AST's Manhattan Servers

Editorial Summary:

  • New Manhattan models are ideal for small and medium-size businesses, including first-time server sites, remote branch offices and larger offices requiring an additional server.
  • AST wraps high-performance functionality into low-cost server solutions complete with Pentium Pro processor power. Available in two configurations, the Manhattan D Series is a high-performance file and print or workgroup server, and the Manhattan Presence Pro is a complete entry-level Internet/Intranet server solution.
  • Combining all the best hardware of the Manhattan D Series with leading Internet software, the Manhattan Presence Pro is a complete Internet server solution. It includes pre-loaded Web server software, Web authoring, E-mail, training software and search engines.
  • Common key hardware features of the Manhattan D Series and the Manhattan Presence Pro include an Intel® Pentium® Pro 200MHz processor, an Intel 440FX chipset, 32MB ECC EDO RAM expandable to 512MB, integrated PCI Ultra Wide SCSI, six 3.5-inch internal drive bays, three 5.25-inch external drive bays, one 3.5-inch floppy disk drive, integrated 10/100 MB Fast Ethernet and Intel LANDesk® Server Manager for comprehensive system management.
  • AST has implemented industry-leading prices for Pentium Pro processor technology, with estimated street prices starting at under $4,000 for the Manhattan D Series, and $5,999 for the Manhattan Presence Pro. Both will begin shipping in December.

LAS VEGAS, Nov. 18, 1996 -- Taking the entry-level server market by storm, AST Computer (NASDAQ:ASTA) today introduces at COMDEX '96 the Manhattan D Series of entry-level workgroup servers and the Manhattan Presence Pro, a complete Internet server solution. Remarkably-priced, AST's new Manhattan servers offer complete solutions at a cost comparable to a high-end desktop. The fully-integrated Manhattan servers have a 200MHz Intel Pentium Pro processor, an Intel 440FX chipset, ten drive bays, abundant RAM memory and are packed with other state-of-the-art hardware. This powerful combination of hardware and technology provides the functionality, power and reliability needed to optimize 32-bit software. With estimated street prices starting at under $4,000, AST's new Manhattan servers confirm AST's commitment to offer an expanded base of server solutions at industry-leading prices.

The Manhattan D Series and the Manhattan Presence Pro are designed for customers who want a powerful server solution, complete with the latest technologies and features, without having to pay a high price. These new Manhattan servers are AST's latest products in the entry-level server segment and were designed to complement the company's current high-end, dual Pentium Pro processor product line.

"The entry-level server market is booming and is targeted as one of the fastest growing segments in the server marketplace. AST has taken the lead by providing low-cost entry-level solutions ideal for first-time server sites, remote branch offices and larger offices requiring an additional server," explained Laura Raybin, director, commercial products group at AST. "Entry-level does not mean low-performance with AST's new Manhattan servers. Any business that runs mission-critical applications and needs a reliable, strong-performance, easy-to-manage solution, will be well suited with the Manhattan D Series or the Manhattan Presence Pro. AST is committed to providing customers and reseller partners with the most comprehensive and complete server solutions available - all at industry-leading price points."

AST is continuing to implement its well-defined server strategy introduced last spring, by providing a complete line of server solutions focused on Pentium Pro processors and Microsoft Windows NT™ Server technologies. AST's Manhattan servers, including the Manhattan Commerce PRO and Manhattan S6200, have been acclaimed by the media for their design, technology implementation and reliability. Recently, the Manhattan Commerce PRO was selected by PC/Computing as a finalist in the "Best Web Server" category for their 1996 MVP Awards, to be announced here at Comdex. The Manhattan D Series and the Manhattan Presence Pro are the latest additions to AST's expanding technology-driven product lines.

Manhattan D Series
AST's Manhattan D Series is a high-performance entry-level server solution complete with a 200MHz Intel Pentium Pro processor and an Intel 440FX chipset for unmatched speed and performance. To provide the power to optimize 32-bit software, the Manhattan D Series has 32MB ECC EDO memory expandable to 512MB, integrated ultra wide SCSI, an 8X CD-ROM drive, 256KB level two cache, four DIMM sites, three PCI slots, two EISA slots, integrated PCI graphics and 100Mbit Ethernet and Intel LANDesk® Server Manager for complete network management.

Designed in a small, convenient form factor, the Manhattan D Series has a minitower chassis with six internal drive bays and a hard drive expansion to support a generous 24GB of storage. The Manhattan D Series is tested, certified and optimized for Novell® NetWare® and IntranetWare®, as well as Microsoft Windows NT Server, so customers can be confident that the network operating systems will operate flawlessly. AST has also bundled Microsoft Windows NT and Novell® NetWare® basic configuration education software by CBT on the Manhattan D Series and the Manhattan Presence Pro, allowing end users to optimize operating system performance.

"Many businesses today are running their network through a high-end desktop computer to compensate for high-priced servers. With the introduction of the new entry-level Manhattan servers, customers can get the performance, availability, reliability and manageability of a high-performance server -- all for the price of a desktop," stated Raybin.

Manhattan Presence Pro
Building upon the powerhouse hardware of the Manhattan D Series, the Manhattan Presence Pro is configured with a complete suite of feature-rich software, creating a true turn-key Internet server solution at an incredible price. Providing all the elements needed to get a business up and running on the World Wide Web in a matter of hours, the Manhattan Presence Pro is pre-installed with Microsoft Windows NT Server, an easy-to-manage yet powerful network operating system with comprehensive file, print and communications services.

"AST's high-end Internet server, the Manhattan Commerce PRO, has been acclaimed by the media and the industry as one of the most complete Internet solutions available," explained Raybin. "Our latest entry-level Internet server, the Manhattan Presence Pro, also provides a complete solution, and is especially configured for customers and resellers who may be new to the Internet and want to enter the market through the means of a low-cost, easy-to-use solution. We have provided that with the Manhattan Presence Pro."

AST's Manhattan Presence Pro includes Merchant Builder Server software from The Internet Factory. This advanced software provides secure Web transaction capability for electronic commerce and protects the Internet server from unwelcome intrusion. Able to support JAVA, Merchant Builder also enables remote administration, multi-homing/hosting and chat rooms.

Also bundled is SoftQuad's HoTMetaL™ Light, which allows home page creation and editing. It supports the HTML standards and Netscape™ Navigator extensions. For an easy connection to the Internet, AST continues its strategic partnership with NETCOM™ On-Line Communication Services, Inc., to provide a complete Internet connectivity component on the Manhattan Presence Pro. In addition, the Manhattan Presence Pro includes the Verity search engine and complete E-mail server software with Software.com's Post.Office™ for E-mail capability both internally and through the Internet.

Pricing, Availability and Warranty
The Manhattan D Series and the Manhattan Presence Pro will begin shipping in December. Keeping with AST's aggressive pricing strategy, estimated street prices for the Manhattan D Series begin at $3,999 and $5,999 for the Manhattan Presence Pro.

Both Manhattan server products carry a three-year on-site limited warranty covering hardware components. An upgraded limited warranty providing four-hour response time is available through the GuardianCareSM program available in the United States.
