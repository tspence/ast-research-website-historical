ASTÒ COMPUTER AMONG THE FIRST TO INTRODUCE

SERVER FOR I2O ARCHITECTURE

 

New AST PremiumÒ HS Server Reduces Cost of Ownership and Provides Peace of Mind

 

 

ATLANTA, Oct. 7, 1997 – AST® Computer, a leading manufacturer of award-winning servers, desktops and mobile computers, today announced here at Networld + Interop its newest mid-range server – the AST PremiumÒ HS Server – combining the latest in high performance and fault tolerance technology with a comprehensive service and support program. Compact, affordable and easy to use, the new server supports I2O technology. In fact, with the Premium HS Server shipping in late October, AST is among the first manufacturer to deliver I2O-ready Ethernet and SCSI RAID controllers as a standard feature in the base server.

"AST is taking aggressive action in the server market. The Premium HS Server platform includes leading-edge technology resulting in lower operational risks, lower cost of ownership, and extended product life to adapt to the needs of today’s growing businesses," said Laura Raybin, AST’s director of Brand Marketing.

The Premium HS Server’s controllers utilize two Intel i960® RD I/O processors (one for the Ethernet controller and smart PCI slots and one for the SCSI RAID controller), to significantly reduce the I/O overhead to the main CPUs, while increasing both Ethernet and hard disk throughput as well as PCI slot acceleration for the future.

 

"With AST being one of the earliest manufacturers of I2O-ready drivers, its customers can take full advantage of the latest in performance enhancement software as it becomes available," said Ron Smith, vice president and general manager of Intel’s Computing Enhancement Group.

Supporting the I2O architecture is only one way that AST is addressing the need for higher- speed server platforms. High-speed 10,000 RPM drives increase overall data access by up to 30 percent, while AST’s patented, impedance-matching SCSI interfacing systems reduce signal noise, increasing both reliability and data throughput.

To ensure optimal performance for its server customers, AST is addressing high data availability in multiple ways: 1) Redundant component designs, which include multiple hot-pluggable power supplies and RAID hard drives; 2) Rapid Mean Time to Repair (MTTR) with the Premium HS Server’s highly serviceable FlexChassis™ architecture simplifying maintenance and upgrades; and 3) Proactive system-wide fault notification with AST’s comprehensive hardware management/monitoring software, which includes thermal sensors, an easy-to-read LCD display, error logs, and chassis intrusion. These features combined, create a highly robust platform with a complete value greater than the individual parts.

Service and Support

AST stands behind its products with a standard three-year limited warranty that includes next-business- day on-site response and a dedicated, toll-free "hot-line" available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. In addition, AST offers Guardian CareSM, an optional, highly responsive support service that includes quick response – four-hour on-site response available 24 hours a day, every day of the year, in the contiguous U.S. – from specially trained technicians. Finally, to convey a complete commitment to customers needs, AST’s comprehensive sales, marketing and technical staff is available for customer comments and questions. AST believes that it is important to listen to the needs of a rapidly changing industry.

Several other support options are available to companies who turn to AST for computing solutions. This includes AST’s web site, which offers Web Support via email as well as answers to common technical questions, software and technical bulletins and an index of files and drivers that can be easily downloaded. The company also offers support through AST Online!, its online bulletin board, which is staffed 24 hours a day, seven days a week, and its telephone bulletin board at (817) 230-6850.

With estimated street prices starting at $7,349, the Premium HS Server offers high value at an extremely low cost. Among the first servers available with I2O-ready architecture, the Premium HS Server is scheduled to be available in late October through AST-authorized resellers.
