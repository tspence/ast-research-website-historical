Announcing New DDS-3 4mm Tape Backup Option Kit

for AST Server Products.

Based on the SonyÒ SDT-9000 mechanism, the DDS-3 DAT drive format is the ideal solution for data backup and recovery in larger local area networks. The DDS-3 DAT drive sets the pace. Typically sixty percent faster than DDS-2 DAT drives, a DDS-3 DAT drive moves data at 2.4 MB/sec. via fast-SCSI 2 interface.

It has a 24 GB capacity and is fully compatible with DDS-1, DDS-2, and DDS-3 cartridges and supports CheyenneÒ , SeagateÒ , StacÒ and other backup software.

With 2.4 MB/sec. transfer rate, enhanced reliability of the Hyper Metal Head technology, and a patented Super Head Cleaner mechanism, this drive is ideally suited to the mid-range and large network server environments. High performance and reliability make the DDS-3 DAT drive a viable cost effective alternative to 8mm and DLT tape drives.

Key Features:

· 24 GB capacity with data compression, 12 GB native. 2.4 MB/sec. sustained data transfer rate (2:1 compression ratio).

· Covered by Guardian CareSM and other AST warranties.

· Includes one 125m Data Media Cartridge and one DAT cleaning cartridge for a complete backup solution, providing ease of operation for the Network Administrator. Includes mounting kit for 5 1/4 in. drive bay.

· Read and write capability-backs up 1 GB of data in 7 minutes. Supports SCSI 1 and SCSI 2 interface.

· Roller guide tape path for gentle tape handling can extend the life of the tape and reduce cost of ownership. Built-in self activated head cleaner.

· Enhanced reliability with implementation of the Hyper Metal Head technology.
