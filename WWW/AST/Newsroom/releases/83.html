AST TEAMS WITH NETCOM TO SIMPLIFY INTERNET CONNECTIVITY WITH NEW MANHATTAN™ SERVER Manhattan Commerce PRO users will be able to save up to $1,000 on initial start-up fees when they sign up for Internet service from NETCOM

IRVINE, Calif. -- June 12, 1996 -- Further broadening the suite of features that will be unveiled during the PC Expo '96 introduction of the Manhattan Commerce PRO Internet server solution, AST Computer today announced a strategic alliance with NETCOM On-Line Communication Services Inc., the leading direct Internet service provider. AST and NETCOM will provide complete Internet connectivity capabilities to Manhattan Commerce PRO resellers and customers. NETCOM offers a simplified approach to setting up Internet connectivity featuring flat-fee pricing geared to different service levels. NETCOM is also offering a one-time discount to Manhattan Commerce PRO customers of up to $1,000 off initial start-up fees.

Slated for a June 17 introduction at PC Expo '96 in New York, AST's Manhattan Commerce PRO is a complete Internet server solution that includes the most advance hardware and software for Internet and Intranet applications.

"Internet access is clearly one of the most important communications issues facing companies today. However, the Internet and Intranet are relatively new concepts for many companies and people are often unsure about the decisions that need to be made when connecting to the 'net," explained Chris Norman, director of marketing for AST's Advanced Systems Group. "By teaming with an internationally recognized Internet service provider like NETCOM, AST has removed the guesswork that often comes with the Internet connectivity territory. Their expertise and proven level of service was a natural pairing with AST and the Manhattan Commerce PRO."

Fast Flexible and Worry Free
"We are pleased to align with AST to deliver an all-in-one Internet server solution," said Ben Slick, Vice President and General Manager of NETCOM's Business Services Group. "Our NetComplete for Business program, a combination of Access, Software, Service, Support and Value, combined with AST's Manhattan Commerce PRO, will allow companies of any size to have a presence on the Web. We believe the Manhattan Commerce PRO will boost communication and commerce via the Internet."

NETCOM is offering specially priced dedicated Internet access to Manhattan Commerce PRO resellers and end users. NETCOM provides a reliable communication link to the Internet using high-speed leased lines at rates ranging from 56KB per second using Frame Relay to 45MB per second (T3).
