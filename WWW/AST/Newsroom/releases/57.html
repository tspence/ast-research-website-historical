AST INCREASES CREDIT LINE TO TOTAL $200 MILLION

IRVINE, Calif., March 8, 1996 -- AST Research Inc. (ASTA-NASDAQ) today announced it has increased its one-year revolving credit facility from $100 million to $200 million.

The $200 million total credit facility is fully available to the company for its working capital needs and is guaranteed by Samsung Electronics, Co., Ltd. pursuant to the previously-announced additional support agreement with Samsung.

Besides increasing the total credit facility to $200 million, the number of banks providing the credit facility has been increased from four to eight; and the credit facility remains agented by Bank of America National Trust and Savings Assoc.

AST Research Inc., a member of the Fortune 500 list of America's largest industrial and service companies, is one of the world's leading personal computer manufacturers. The $2.468 billion company develops a broad spectrum of desktop, mobile and server PC products that are sold in more than 100 countries worldwide.
