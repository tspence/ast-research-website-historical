AST®COMPUTER GIVES SERVER USERS WHAT THEY WANT:
NEXT BUSINESS DAY SUPPORT

 

Provided As A Standard Offering To All Server Products,
AST Delivers The Service That The Industry Demands

 

IRVINE, Calif., August 13, 1997 -- Committed to stand by its server customers and their mission-critical business needs, AST Computer today announced it has upgraded its standard server support offering to include Next Business Day On-Site service for all server customers. Recognizing that any system downtime can have a great impact on conducting business, AST is committed to delivering more value for each server owners' investment.


"It is clear that today's customer wants to partner with a company that stands by them and their business, and can deliver more than just a box," stated David Tordoff, director of AST's Service and Support Operation. "By providing Next Day On-Site service and a dedicated toll-free technical support hotline as part of our standard terms, AST is delivering assurance to network administrators that their complex server environments are backed by support designed to keep their systems up and running."
AST understands the needs of the small to medium-sized business owner and believes that providing this improved service offering will greatly benefit customers by reducing potential system downtime and increasing business productivity, thereby improving the customer's business.

Watching Over Customers With Guardian Care
In addition to providing Next Business Day support to server customers, AST also offers Guardian Care, an optional upgrade package available with AST's Manhattan™ family of server products that ensures the maximum level of support for AST servers. This highly-responsive service offerings includes Four-Hour On-Site Response, Continual Coverage, Guardian Certified Technicians and access to Advanced Technical Resources.

Service and Support
AST server products come with a three-year on-site limited warranty covering hardware components.
AST backs all of its products with a host of multi-faceted support programs including a dedicated toll-free "hot" line for users in the United States, available 24 hours-a-day, seven days-a-week. Users can also tap the electronic bulletin board service for a myriad of information (1-817-230-6850).
In addition, service and technical support information is posted on the AST World Wide Web site, including Web Support, a feature that allows users to e-mail questions from the Web site directly to the AST online support team. Other Web site service elements include a list of the most frequently asked technical questions, software and technical bulletins and an index of files and drivers that can be easily downloaded. Information about AST, its products and services can be found on the World Wide Web at http://www.ast.com.
