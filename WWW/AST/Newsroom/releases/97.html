Novell® NetWare® 4.1 SMP Now Certified on
Manhattan P5100 and P5133

Stepping up to the challenge of meeting increased throughput and greater system performance needs in growing network environments, AST has certified Novell NetWare® 4.1 SMP on the Manhattan P5100 and P5133.

Symmetric multiprocessing (SMP) is the latest addition to the NetWare 4 network operating system that provides scalability for application services. The scalability of AST Manhattan servers works in sync with NetWare SMP to accommodate increased users on the network and more resource-intensive applications.
