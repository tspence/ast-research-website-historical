Other Information

Is it possible to update the modem's speed with flash software?

At this time there is not flashable update software available for the modem.

Is there a UART on the modem card?

The PCtel PCT288I chipset fills the role of the DSP data pump, UART, and controller on this modem. The modem comes with a high throughput virtual UART.
