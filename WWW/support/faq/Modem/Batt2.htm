Manufacturer and Chipset

The modem was designed specifically for AST by Boca Research, and it is primarily identified in the Windows® 95 Device Manager as a FDSP34AI modem. This modem uses a HSM288XCF V.34 chipset designed and manufactured by AT&T Microelectronics. The V.34 chipset is one of the more common chipsets on the market, and it meets International Telecommunication Union (ITU) standards.
