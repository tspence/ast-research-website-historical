Manufacturer and Chipset

The modem was designed specifically for AST by Boca Research, and it is primarily identified in the Windows® 95 Device Manager as an FDVSP modem. The modem uses a V.34 chipset designed and manufactured by Rockwell International's semiconductor devision. The V.34 chipset is one of the more common chipsets on the market, and it meets International Telecommunication Union (ITU) standards. The two different versions offer speeds of 28.8Kbps or 33.6Kbps.
