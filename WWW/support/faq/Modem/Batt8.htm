Reinstallation Procedures

You can use the following procedure to reset the modem in your system:

1. Double-click on the System icon in the Windows Control Panel.

2. Click on the Device Manager tab.

3. Remove all entries from the Ports (COM & LPT) and Modems sections.

4. Restart the computer and the system will redetect the ports.

5. Make the driver diskettes for the AT&T 28.8 Modem Drivers v4.10 12/13/95 (for Boca) from the AST Disk Utility program.

or

Download the following file from our Web site:

BATTDRV.EXE Win95 v4.27 Boca AT&T Modem

Follow these directions to install the port driver for the AT&T Boca:

1. Open Add New Hardware under Control Panel.

2. Do not let Windows® 95 auto detect the hardware. Choose Ports (COM & LPT) and then Have Disk. On the Copy manufacturer’s files from: line, select the floppy drive.

3. Ensure that Communications Port is highlighted. Click Next and finish the procedure. Restart the computer.

Follow this procedure to change the port settings:

1. Double-click on Communications Port in Device Manager, and then click on the Resources tab. Ensure that the settings are as follows:

Input/Output Range: 02F8-02FF
Interrupt Request : 3

2. Double-click on Communications Port in Device Manager. Change the driver by clicking on the Driver tab and then the Change Driver... button. Select Show All Devices to choose the MV.34I or FDSP34A COM Port Alias.

3. Select COM2, click Properties, and change the baud rate from 9600 to 57600. Click OK or Close.

4. Access the Modems section of Control Panel, and choose Add New Modem.

Do not let Windows® 95 detect the modem.

5. Click on Have Disk and then select the floppy drive.

6. Choose 28.8kbps V.34 FDSP34AI on COM2. If prompted for the location of the Windows® 95 disks, enter C:/WINDOWS/OPTIONS/CABS.
