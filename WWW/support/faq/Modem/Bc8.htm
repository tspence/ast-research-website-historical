Reinstallation Procedures

You can use the following procedure to reset the modem in your system:

Removing the existing drivers:

1. Double-click on the System icon in the Windows Control Panel.

2. Click on the Device Manager tab.

3. Remove all entries from the Ports (COM & LPT) and Modems sections.

Configuring the COM port:

1. Access Add New Hardware from Control Panel.

2. Do not let Windows® 95 automatically detect the hardware. Choose Ports (COM & LPT) and then click Next.

3. Select Standard Port Types under the Manufacturers list and then click Next>.

4. Ensure that Communications Port is highlighted, and click Next>.

5. Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the installation. Do not restart the computer when prompted.

6. Access the Ports (COM & LPT) section of Device Manager.

7. Click on Communications Port (COM2), and then click Properties.

8. Change the Resource Usage to the following:

Input/Output Range: 02F8-02FF
Interrupt Request: 03

9. Click on OK or Close, and then restart the computer.

Installing the modem drivers:

To complete this procedure, you need the modem driver disk from the AST Disk Utility.

1. Access the Modems icon in Control Panel.

2. Do not let Windows automatically detect the modem.

3. Insert the modem driver disk, and choose the Have Disk option.

4. Ensure that the Boca modem is selected and click Next.

5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
