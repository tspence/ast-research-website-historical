How do I create a new desktop view in AST Works versions 2.00 and 2.10?

You can follow three sets of procedures to create a new desktop view from a blank desktop view:

Steps 1-8 Creating the new desktop view.
Steps 9-16 Adding the view buttons for the existing desktop views (Main, Productivity, etc.) onto the new desktop view.
Steps 17-24 Adding a view button for the new desktop view onto the existing desktop views.

Follow steps 1-8 to create a new desktop view:

  1. Insert the Advantage! Data Disc in the CD-ROM drive.
  2. Click on View on the taskbar, and select Home View from the pull-down menu. Select Go To from the expanded menu. You should see the Desktop - Main screen.
  3. Click on View on the taskbar, and choose Create... from the pull-down menu.

    You should see the Graphics Manager - Desktop.

  4. Click on BLANK from the list of choices, and then press <ENTER>.

    You should see a screen labeled Desktop - Untitled.

  5. Click on View on the taskbar, and choose Home View from the pull-down menu. Select Go To from the expanded menu.
  6. Click on the Yes button when you are asked if you want to save the changes.

    You should see the Add a Picture to the List screen.

  7. Enter the desired information in the "Name:" and "Description:" fields..
  8. Click on the OK button when you finish entering the information. You should see the Desktop - Main screen.

    Now you can add a button for your new desktop view on the other AST Works screens (Main, Productivity, etc.). You can follow steps 9-16 to add the button.
  9. Go to the desktop (e.g. Desktop - Main) where you want to add a button for your new desktop.
  10. Click on View on the taskbar, and choose Design... from the pull-down menu. AST Works is now in design mode. You can make changes to the desktop in this mode.
  11. Click on Add on the taskbar, and then choose View Button... from the pull-down menu.

    12. You should see the Graphics Manager - Desktop.

  12. Click on the name of your new desktop view from the list of screens, and press <ENTER>.
  13. Use the mouse to drag and drop the new button to the desired location.

    NOTE: If any items overlap on the desktop, the top item will be the visible item. However, every time you click on this item you will actually be selecting the bottom item.

  14. Click on File on the taskbar, and then choose Exit from the pull-down menu.
  15. Click on View on the taskbar, and then choose Home View from the pull-down menu. Select Go To from the expanded menu.
  16. Click on the Yes button when you are asked if you want to save the changes.

    Repeat steps 9-16 to add buttons to other screens

    Now you can add buttons for other AST Works screens (Main, Productivity, etc.) onto your new screen. Follow steps 17-24 to complete this procedure.

  17. Go to your new desktop screen.
  18. Click on View on the taskbar, and then choose Design... from the menu. Again, AST Works is in the design mode.
  19. Click on Add the taskbar, and then choose View Button... from the pull-down menu. You should see the Graphics Manager - Desktop.
  20. Click on the name of the button you want to add to your screen, and press <ENTER>.
  21. Use the mouse to drag and drop the button to the desired location the new desktop.

    NOTE: If any items overlap on the desktop, the top item will be the visible item. However, every time you click on this item you will actually be selecting the bottom item.

    Repeat steps 17-21 to add additional buttons to the screen. When you finish, go to step 22.

  22. Click on File, and then choose Exit from the pull-down menu.
  23. Click on View on the taskbar, and then choose Home View from the pull-down menu. Select Go To from the expanded menu.
  24. Click on the Yes button when you are asked if you want to save the changes. You should see the Desktop - Main screen.

How do I create a new desktop view in AST Works version 2.11?

You can follow three sets of procedures to create a new desktop view from a blank desktop view:

Steps 1-6 Creating the new desktop view.
Steps 7-11 Adding the view buttons for the existing desktop views (Main, Productivity, etc.) onto the new desktop view.
Steps 12-18 Adding a view button for the new desktop view onto the existing desktop views.

Follow steps 1-6 to create the new desktop view:

  1. Insert the Advantage! Data Disc in the CD-ROM drive.
  2. Click on View from the taskbar, and choose Home View from the menu. Select Go To from the expanded menu.

    You should see the Desktop - Main menu.

  3. Click on View on the taskbar, and choose Create from the pull-down menu.

    You should see the Graphics Manager - Desktop.

  4. Click to highlight BLANK from the menu, and then press <ENTER>.

    You should see a new Desktop - Untitled screen.
  5. Enter the desired information in the "Name:" and "Description:" fields.
  6. Click on the OK button when you complete your entries. The screen Desktop - Untitled should change to the name you entered in the "Name:" field.

    Now you can add buttons for other desktop views (Main, Productivity, etc...) to your new desktop. Follow steps 7-11 to add buttons to your new desktop.

    Click on View, and then choose Design... from the pull-down menu. AST Works is now in design mode.

  7. Click on Add from the taskbar, and then choose View Button... from the pull-down menu.

    You should see the Graphics Manager - Desktop screen.

  8. Select the screen where you want to add the view button, and press <ENTER>.
  9. Use your mouse to drag and drop the new view button to a desired location.

    NOTE: If any items overlap on the desktop, the top item will be the visible item. However, every time you click on this item you will actually be selecting the bottom item.

    Repeat steps 7-9 to add buttons for other desktop views to your new desktop view. When you finish adding buttons, go to step 10.

  10. Click on File, and choose Exit from the pull-down menu.
  11. Click on the Yes button to save the current changes and exit the design mode.

    Now you can add a button for your new desktop view to other AST Works views (Main, Productivity, etc...). Follow steps 12-18 to add a button for the new desktop view to other AST Works screens.

  12. Go to the screen (e.g. Desktop - Main) where you want to add a button for your new desktop view.
  13. Click on View on the taskbar, and then choose Design... from the pull-down menu. Now, AST Works is in design mode.
  14. Click on Add on the menu bar, and then choose View Button... from the pull-down menu. You should see the Graphics Manager - Desktop screen.
  15. Click on the name of your new desktop view, and press <ENTER>.
  16. Use the mouse to drag and drop the new button to the desired location on the desktop.
  17. Click on File, and then choose Exit from the pull-down menu.
  18. Click on the Yes button when asked to save current changes.

    Repeat step 12-18 to add the new button to the other desktop views.

How do I install a new application in the AST Works environment?

Although your AST Advantage! computer shipped with a wide variety of software, AST Works will grow as your needs and productivity increases. AST Works is compatible with most Windows-based software on the market. You can follow this procedure to install a new application in AST Works:

  1. Click on the view button (e.g. My Software, Communications, Productivity etc...) where you want to install the new program.
  2. Click on File on the AST Works taskbar, and choose Install... from the pull-down menu.
  3. Enter the name of the installation utility. If you are unsure of what filename to enter, consult the application's documentation. Typically, most programs will use "setup" or "install." You can also use the Browse... button to locate the file.
  4. Click on OK to register your selection. Follow any additional steps in the program's installation shield.

For more information on installing applications, see the following online Help topics:

  • Install another program
  • Add application to desktop

How do I create a new icon in AST Works?

As you add programs to your computer, you may want to update your AST Works desktop. Follow this procedure to create an icon in AST Works:

  1. Click on the view button (e.g. My Software, Communications, Productivity etc...) where you want to create the icon.
  2. Click on View on the AST Works Taskbar, and choose Design from the pull-down menu. You can also start "design mode" by clicking on the T-square icon on the left side of the screen. The icon will appear as a U-turn arrow until you exit "design mode."
  3. Click on the Add... button on the AST Works Taskbar, and then choose Program... from the pull-down menu.
  4. Fill in the appropriate "Title:," "Command Line:," and "Working Directory:" fields. You can use the Browse... button to search for these fields in your system.
  5. Use the "Current Graphic" to select the appropriate icon for the application.

When I try to access the Communications view in AST Works, the Entertainment view pops up instead. Did I do something wrong?

The Communications view may be corrupt. This problem often occurs when the Design button is not pressed after you create or move icons within AST Works. To resolve this problem, first run the SCANDISK and DEFRAG utilities from the DOS prompt to clean up any hard drive errors.

Next, follow these steps to replace the corrupt files. You will need to have the AST Works Data Disc in the CD-ROM drive before you begin the procedure:

  1. Exit all software including AST Works and Windows.
  2. Enter "D:" at the DOS prompt.
  3. Enter the following command at the D:\> prompt:
    CD\ORIGINALS\ASTWORKS\PICTURES\DESKTOP
  4. Enter the following command:
    XCOPY COMM.DTP C:\ASTWORKS\PICTURES\DESKTOP
  5. Answer <Y> to overwrite the files. When you enter this command, your computer will copy the original Communications view from the AST Works Data Disc to the hard drive. Any icons that you have added will need to be re-created.

If you need to reset one of the other views in AST Works, you can substitute one of the following files for COMM.DTP:

  • Main - MAIN.DTP
  • Productivity - PROD.DTP
  • System Tools - SYST.DTP
  • Reference - REFE.DTP
  • Entertainment - ENTE.DTP

How do I restore the original Views in AST Works?

If you ever need to restore the original views in AST Works, you can copy them from the AST Works CD.

Follow this procedure to restore the original views in AST Works:

  1. Exit AST Works and Windows.
  2. Insert your AST Works CD into the CD-ROM drive.
  3. Enter the following commands at the C:\> prompt:
    D:\
    CD\ORIGINAL\ASTWORKS\PICTURES\DESKTOP
  4. Enter the following command:
    XCOPY *.DTP C:\ASTWORKS\PICTURES\DESKTOP
  5. Enter "CD.." after the computer stops copying the files.
  6. Enter the following command:
    XCOPY *.DB0 C:\ASTWORKS\PICTURES
  7. Enter "C:" after the computer completely copies the files.
  8. Enter "WIN" at the C:\> prompt to return to Windows.

How do I reset AST Works?

After you run the AST Works desktop for a period of time, you may need to reset this shell application. Before you begin this procedure, we recommend that you run the SCANDISK and DEFRAG utilities to clear up any errors that may already exist on the hard drive.

Before you reset AST Works, please note that any icons you may have created will need to be established again. Also, you will have to reconfigure Phone Center. Follow this procedure to reset AST Works:

  1. Exit all software, including Windows.
  2. Insert the AST Works Data Disc into your CD-ROM drive.
  3. Enter the following command at the C:\> prompt:
    CD\ASTWORKS
  4. Enter the following command at the C:\ASTWORKS> prompt:
    ATTRIB -R *.* /S
  5. Enter the following command:
    XCOPY D:\ORIGINAL\ASTWORKS C:\ASTWORKS /S
  6. Press <A> when you see the message, "Overwrite (yes, no, all)."
  7. Enter the following commands when the system finishes copying the files:
    CD\
    WIN

This procedure should take about 15 minutes.

When I use the speakerphone, I can hear the person calling in, but they cannot hear me. What's the problem?

The most common cause of this problem is a wiring error. Make sure that the wires are plugged in properly on the back of the system. Double-check the following items:

  • The phone line from the wall should plug into the Line slot on the back of the modem.
  • The microphone should plug into the Mic-in slot on the back of the modem
  • The cable that connects location J5 on the modem should connect to location JP15 on the sound card.

Make sure to answer the phone with the speaker phone software before Phone Center answers the line. If you wait until Phone Center answers the line, then disable Phone Center and answer with the speakerphone. Additionally, once you "turn on" the speakerphone, be sure to check the volume settings in the Creative Mixer.
