Business Desktop Year 2000 Tables

The table below describes the action you should take for AST desktop systems to prepare for the year 2000.

Manual Update: Enter the date at a DOS prompt to enable year 2000 compliance.
Power Off Rollover: Manually enter the date at a DOS prompt, turn off the computer, and then reboot the computer.
No Action Required: The computer shipped with year 2000 compliance.
TSR: Download the Terminate and Stay Resident Fix.
