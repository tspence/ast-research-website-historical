What is AST LifeLine?

AST-LifeLine automates the service process, making technical support easier to obtain. By incorporating SystemSoft’s TalkShopä software and VoiceViewä modem technology into your AST Advantage! system, AST is providing a whole new way for you to obtain support. SystemSoft’s TalkShop is powerful software that takes advantage of your modem's VoiceView® feature. SystemSoft’s new technology lets you talk and exchange computer information during an ordinary telephone call by automatically muting voice transmission momentarily while data is transferred. With VoiceView TalkShop, a phone, and your computer, you can get information in a whole new way. You can give someone your electronic business card, send and receive files quickly and easily, or play games with someone at another location. And since VoiceView TalkShop works over a single line phone connection, any transactions you make are fast and secure.

How do I start VoiceView TalkShop?

VoiceView TalkShop is easy to use and ready when you are. Just follow these steps:

  1. Look in the "Getting Started" Manual to make sure that your modem and telephone are hooked up properly.
  2. Click on the ASTLineLine icon located on your desktop or the start menu.
  3. Click on the ASTLineLine billboard on the rightside of the VoiceView TalkShop window.
  4. Fill out the Customer Information Form.
  5. Click on Dial to connect to AST LifeLine Technical Support.
  6. Follow the prompts to find a solution to your problem.
  7. Hang up the speakerphone (if no solution is found)
  8. Exit out of the system and you will be connected to a Support Representative.

What services does Lifeline offer me?

AST LifeLine offers a whole new way for you to obtain support from AST. One connection lets you talk to a representative, download files and view information. That's because AST LifeLine is a revolutionary application for integrating voice and computer data across a single ordinary phone line. What is SystemWizard?

SystemWizard is a Windows 95 software product that shipped on the latest series of Advantage!® computers. SystemWizard automatically detects, diagnoses, and resolves hardware and software problems. When you encounter a problem, the SystemWizard Client will attempt to identify the problem and fix it. If SystemWizard Client is unable to resolve a problem because it doesn't have enough information, the application will attempt to connect with a phone line to a remote SystemWizard Server. If the SystemWizard client is still unable to identify a solution, you will be instructed to contact technical support.

How do I use SystemWizard?

SystemWizard attempts to diagnose any type of PC problem. When you experience a problem, follow these steps:

  1. Double click on the SystemWizard icon located on the desktop. The SystemWizard desktop will display a graphical representation of potential problem areas, such as hardware, software, printer, modem, monitor, etc.
  2. Select a problem area from the SystemWizard desktop where you think the problem is occurring. It is not critical for you to select the correct problem area. SystemWizard can still make the correct diagnosis even if the problem area you select is not where the problem exists.
  3. Select an answer from the list of symptoms. Based on your answers, SystemWizard will gather system-level data that is helpful in diagnosing and solving the problem. You may be asked additional questions before SystemWizard makes a diagnosis.

If SystemWizard cannot find the problem, the application will ask if you want to attach to an AST SystemWizard Server. If you choose "Yes" the SystemWizard will use the dial-up networking features on your system to connect to the server. If SystemWizard still cannot solve your problem after it connects with the remote server and updates its local information, you should contact your Technical Support representative for the product(s) that you believe contain the problem.
