Advantage! 9315 and 9316 Gaming PCs.

Superior family entertainment with superb multimedia and gaming capabilities is at your fingertips
with the new Advantage! 9315 and 9316 gaming PCs. The gaming systems come complete with rich
multimedia content and games that feature compelling high-resolution 3-dimensional realism, S3 ViRGE
graphics and video acceleration, 3-D sound, 24-watt Altec Lansing speakers and 40-watt subwoofer,
and a Logitech Wingman Extreme Joystick. Powerful 166MHz and 200MHz Pentium processors, large
hard drives and 32MB RAM round out this spectacular gaming experience.
