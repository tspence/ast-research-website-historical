ASCENTIA™ A SERIES PLUS
Models A60/A70
The Smart Notebook For Home And Office. 











The AST tradition of providing the best in value and performance continues with the Ascentia A Series Plus.  With features like a 150 MHz Intel© Pentium© processor or a 150 MHz Intel Pentium processor with MMX™ technology, a 12.1" SVGA TFT Active-Matrix Screen with 2 MB video RAM, a 1.44 GB Hard Drive and 256 KB Level-2 cache, the Ascentia A is well equipped to handle any of your mobile computing needs.  















Key Features:



    

    

  • 150MHz Intel® Pentium® processor or 150MHz Intel Pentium processor with MMX™ technology and 256KB Level-2 cache

  • Large Super VGA 12.1" diagonal TFT Active-Matrix color screen

  • 16MB EDO DRAM, expandable to 80MB

  • Switch between a 10X Maximum Speed CD-ROM and a floppy drive in the AST "SmartBay"

  • Full motion video with built-in MPEG playback software or add an MPEG playback PC Card to be used with the built-in Zoomed Video port

  • Ultra-fast performance with the built-in 32-bit Card Bus architecture for PC Card slots

  • Optional Mini-Dock for one-step connectivity

  • Pre-loaded 33.6 kbps fax/modem PC Card (model A70 Plus in U.S. and Canada only)

  • Wide palm rest, Windows® 95 keyboard and SmartPoint™ touchpad pointing device

  • Duracell DR36 NiMh battery compatible
