Windows NT 4.0 - Other Information
Item #1 Since the Mini-Dock does not have a Cardbus Controller, Cardbus Cards are not supported in the Mini-Dock.
Item #2 Ositech card did not configure on IRQ 10 or 7. Would configure on IRQ 3.
Item #3 Card not recognized by System Soft after loading driver. Card will not configure.
Item #4 The Xircom CEM56-100BTX card requires version 3.10.04 of Cardwizard. Contact AST support if you are using this card.

Item #5

The card will configure and you are able to use the Modem, but the LAN portion will not allow you to browse the network.
