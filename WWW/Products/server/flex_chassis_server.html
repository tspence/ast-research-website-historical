Amazing New Server FlexChassis Architecture Combines Easy Maintenance With Unparalleled Performance

The key to this innovative product is AST's exclusive NLX Server FlexChassis. The top, front and both sides of this chassis are removable to allow simple and complete access to the interior. All boards and subsystems are easily removable for quick replacement and scalability. Not only does this serviceable architecture save you time and lower your total cost of ownership, but the compact packaging makes it perfect for any 19" industry standard rack cabinet. And don't worry about the rack complicating matters. Because of its top-entry layout and front accessible drive bays, this is one server that is always easy to service no matter where you keep it.
