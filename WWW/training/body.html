AST understands the importance of staying up to date, both on AST products and new business trends. With this in mind, AST developed a series of training programs for Ovation! Resellers that addresses all business needs.

The business world is changing; companies need to adapt to new environments to keep their competitive edge. Companies that thrive in the future will need skilled executives in new management styles and sales people expert in negotiating, persuading and building long-term customer relationships. The Business Skills Workshops are designed to give you the tools to stay one step ahead in business.

In today's commodity market it is becoming more difficult to differentiate your products and services to your customers. AST has developed a workshop called "How To Value Sell Commodity Product" which delivers techniques on how to help you "win" more business.

The AST Ovation! Program also includes comprehensive technical training on all AST products. Technical training is a self-paced program packaged with on-site repair labs to give your representatives a thorough training on AST products.
