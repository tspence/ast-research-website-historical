 AST developed the Demo Unit Program to assist the resellers sales efforts by offer AST systems at a special purchase price. The Demo Unit Program allows the reseller to offer the Demo Unit to an end user as an evaluation unit or to use as a display model.

AST Ovation! Demo Unit Program Overview

  • Authorized AST Resellers may purchase two system configurations for each authorized sales locations, from each product family (i.e. One Bravo LC Series, Bravo MS Series, Bravo MS-T Series, Bravo MS-L Series, etc.) at a special demo unit price, through the current life of the product.
  • The reseller must keep the demo unit(s) in inventory for a continuous period of four months starting immediately after delivery by AST. The demo unit may not be resold, donated, rented, leased or transferred in any manner during the four month period.
  • After the four month period the reseller may sell the Demo Unit so long as the product is not represented as "new."
  • The reseller must purchase the demo unit directly from AST. Demo products may not be pulled from the resellers inventory.
  • Resellers may use either FlexFunds or AST Allowance funds for payment.
  • Resellers must complete the Online Demo Unit Request Form.
  • A manager for officer of the company is to be the "authorizing signature" who will take responsibility for the purchase of the AST demo unit.
  • AST Product purchased through the Demo Unit program is not eligible for Price Protection or Stock Rotation.
  • AST will select the freight carrier for Demo Unit orders. Demo Units are shipped by surface transportation. Standard lead times apply to Demo Unit Purchases.
  • Appropriate shippings costs and insurance will be added to the purchase price by AST.
