Meeting the challenge of increasing product sales growth from quarter to quarter take considerable commitment and energy. We believe this hard work deserves recognition and reward and have developed a Volume Incentive Rebate to reward you when your quarterly sales of AST products grows. As your sales of AST product grows, you can earn rebates of up to 2% of your net quarterly sales.

 Growth  Rebate
 25% - 34.9%  1%
 35% - 44.9%  1.5%
 45% and up  2%

Terms and Conditions

  • An automated sales-out report must be provided to AST quarterly to verify total sales-out against the target.
  • The rebate program applies to all AST commercial products you are currently authorized to sell.
  • All products must be purchased through an Authorized AST Distributor.
  • AST reserves the right to cancel this program at any time without advance notice
  • All rebates will be paid by check within 30 days of the end of the quarter in which your goal was achieved.
