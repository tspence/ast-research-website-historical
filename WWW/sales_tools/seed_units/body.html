The ability to review and evaluate new systems is crucial to the sales cycle. AST's Gold resellers have made the strongest commitment to AST. To make the sales cycle easier, a special evaluation unit program has been established for their needs.

Evaluation Unit Program Overview

  • Authorized Ovation! Gold Resellers are eligible to receive three systems (one per product family) during each fiscal quarter.
  • AST Ovation! Gold Resellers receive evaluation unit systems for a 60-day free evaluation. At the end of the 60-day evaluation, the reseller may either return the system to AST, or purchase at a significant discount.
  • Evaluation Unit systems may be used for reseller technical evaluation or may be placed with end users for technical evaluation.
  • During 60-day free evaluation period, title and ownership of the equipment shall remain with AST.
  • Upon purchase of the evaluation unit system, AST's standard product warranty shall apply to all evaluation unit systems.
  • AST will pay all shipping charges to and from reseller's ship to address.


Ordering Procedure

  • To order, the Ovation! Gold Reseller must call Customer Sales Administration at (714) 727-8853 or (714) 727-8867 for pricing information. Completed order forms may be faxed to (714) 727-7630.
  • A purchase order including payment information for the cost of the systems, dated 60 days after receipt of the system must accompany the order form. Evaluation Unit orders will not be processed without an accompanying purchase order and payment information.
  • Ovation! Gold Resellers may use either FlexFunds, or credit card for payment. Payment type must be specified on Evaluation Unit Order Form and accompanying purchase order.


Evaluation Unit Return Procedures

  • The Evaluation Unit systems Return Authorization Number with shipping instructions and due date to contact AST are included in the shipping container.
  • To purchase the system, the Ovation! Gold Reseller must contact Customer Sales Administration to verify purchase and confirm purchase order information.
  • If the Evaluation Unit system is not received within 30 days of return date, the Ovation! Gold Reseller will be charged for the system.
