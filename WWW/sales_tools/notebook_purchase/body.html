Sales Force Automation Program
AST understands the value of arming your sales force with AST notebooks for product sales demonstrations. When your sales reps uses an AST notebook, the message is clear, AST is the preferred system of the sales representative.

AST has developed a program designed to arm your sales force with AST notebooks plus options at a special price.

Program Overview

  • Each AST Ovation! Reseller may purchase up to 15 AST notebooks and appropriate options in a twelve-month period.
  • The Ovation! Reseller is responsible for all freight and insurance charges.
  • The AST Ovation! Reseller Sales Rep must be classified as a full-time employee at the time of the purchase.
  • The AST Ovation! Reseller Sales Rep must retain ownership of the notebook for a period of six months. The system may not be resold, donated, rented, leased or transferred in any manner during that period.
  • Purchases are not subject to sales tax exemption.
  • Products purchased through the Sales Force Automation Program are not eligible for Price Protection or Stock Rotation.

Ordering Procedure

  • Complete the Online Notebook Purchase Request Form.
  • The Reseller Sales Representative may contact Customer Sales Administration at (714) 727-8859 or FAX completed order forms to (714) 727-7630 along with a photocopy of the employee's payroll stub to verify employment. (It is not necessary to show the employee's salary.)
  • Indicate method of payment on the completed form (cashier's check, money order or credit card).
