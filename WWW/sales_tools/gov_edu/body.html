AST just made it easier to receive the Government and Education rebate. Now resellers may receive the rebate instantly when ordering AST products from a participating AST National Channel Account. All you need is a valid purchase order from an eligible government agency or educational institution at the time of the order.

Program Overview

  • AST resellers who sell specific AST desktop, mobile and server systems to an eligible education institution, or government agency, will receive an instant rebate directly from a participating AST Authorized National Channel Account at the time of purchase, based on the current rebate amount at the of purchase.
  • Once the rebate has been requested by the reseller under this rebate program, systems are not eligible for stock rotation, or Allowance Fund payment to the reseller.

Eligible Educational Institutions

  • Eligible educational institutions must be accredited, non-profit, tax exempt public or private, full time K-12th grade schools, four-year colleges and universities, two year colleges, vocational and technical schools.
  • For questions regarding eligible educational institutions, contact your AST Inside Sales Representative.

Eligible Government Agencies

  • Eligible government agencies include branches of the Federal, State and Local governments.
  • For questions regarding eligible government agencies, contact your AST Inside Sales Representative.

Government/Education Rebate Procedure:

  • AST resellers must submit a copy of the purchase order from an eligible government agency or educational institution to the AST Authorized National Channel Account at the time of purchase.
  • The reseller order must exactly match the purchase order from the government agency or educational institution. In the case of a discrepancy, the instant rebate will be issued based on the quantity specified on the purchase order from the government agency or educational institution.

Current rebates may be found in the product section.
